9-2-2023 NHL All-Star Skill Competitions
The annual All-Star event kicked off today with the skill competitions. Team North America and Team World competed in six different races and the fans got to see their stars shine.

Team World won the puck control relay with a team of Rasmus Dahlin, Erik Karlsson and Nicklas Bäckström.

Connor McDavid of Team North America won the fastest skater competition, beating Erik Karlsson in the final.

Roman Josi of Team World won the hardest shot competition with a result of 88 MPH, beating Charlie Coyle in the final.

Steven Stamkos of Team North America won the accuracy shooting competition, beating Teuvo Teravainen in the final.

Robin Lehner of Team World won the goalie competition (pass&score), beating Evan Fitzpatrick in the final.

Team North America won the breakaway relay with a team of Connor McDavid, Sidney Crosby and Steven Stamkos.

The yearly clash between Team North America and Team World will take place tomorrow.
