10-9-2021 Player of the week: Gabriel Landeskog (L)
NHL has awarded Gabriel Landeskog (L) of Colorado Avalanche as Player Of The Week He registered 2 goals and 4 assists for 6 points in 2 games last week.

A native of Sweden, Gabriel Landeskog was originally drafted to the NHL by the Colorado Avalanche in the 1st round of the 2011 entry draft. He has appeared in 758 NHL games during his career, notching 216 goals and adding 310 assists along with 631 penalty minutes. The 28 year old left wing has played 2 games with the Colorado Avalanche this season, totalling 2+4=6 with 5 PIM.
