10-5-2019 Player of the week: Sidney Crosby (C)
NHL has awarded Sidney Crosby (C) of Tampa Bay Lightning as Player Of The Week He registered 3 goals and 3 assists for 6 points in 4 games last week. He also had 1 game winning goal.

A native of Canada, Sidney Crosby was originally drafted to the NHL by the Pittsburgh Penguins in the 1st round of the 2005 entry draft. He has appeared in 945 NHL games during his career, notching 445 goals and adding 709 assists along with 690 penalty minutes. The 31 year old center has played 82 games with the Tampa Bay Lightning this season, totalling 33+30=63 with 56 PIM.
