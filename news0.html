8-6-2021 San Jose Sharks celebrate Stanley Cup
The Stanley Cup Champions San Jose Sharks were celebrated in their hometown with grand festivities today. The Stanley Cup was driven through the town in a parade and the fans were chanting the team's name every time captain Joe Pavelski lifted the Cup above his head.

The mayor of the town greeted the players on a stage with the media surrounding them as masses of loyal San Jose Sharks fans chanted the names of Joe Pavelski and Martin Jones.

It's just beautiful...I'm out of words, commented Joe Pavelski to a local reporter.
7-6-2021 Playoff news
San Jose Sharks have won their series against the Philadelphia Flyers. They have been awarded $4,000,000 as prize money.
