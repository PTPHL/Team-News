25-6-2019 Tyson Baillie frustrated
New Jersey Devils right wing Tyson Baillie feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a regular player.

A native of Canada, Tyson Baillie, was originally signed as a free agent to the NHL. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 23 year old right wing is getting ready for the upcoming season.
