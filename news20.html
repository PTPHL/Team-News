13-3-2020 Torey Krug reaches a milestone !
Torey Krug scored the 300th point of his career in tonight's game against the Colorado Avalanche on a goal by Brayden Schenn. I just want to thank all the people that have supported me throughout my career. I couldn't have done this without them, he read from a prepared statement..

A native of USA, Torey Krug, was originally signed as a free agent to the NHL. He has appeared in 554 NHL games during his career, notching 61 goals and adding 239 assists along with 222 penalty minutes. The 28 year old defenseman has played 68 games with the New Jersey Devils this season, totalling 9+27=36 with 16 PIM.
