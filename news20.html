8-11-2021 Brayden Schenn reaches a milestone !
Brayden Schenn scored the 200th goal of his career in tonight's game against the Chicago Blackhawks on a feed by Conor Timmins. As long as the team wins I'm happy. The personal records don't mean anything to me if they aren't a part of winning the Cup, he said after the game in the dressing room.

A native of Canada, Brayden Schenn was originally drafted to the NHL by the Los Angeles Kings in the 1st round of the 2009 entry draft. He has appeared in 773 NHL games during his career, notching 200 goals and adding 253 assists along with 443 penalty minutes. The 30 year old center has played 13 games with the New Jersey Devils this season, totalling 2+5=7 with 17 PIM.
