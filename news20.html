8-6-2021 Alex DeBrincat frustrated
New Jersey Devils left wing Alex DeBrincat feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a regular player.

A native of USA, Alex DeBrincat was originally drafted to the NHL by the Chicago Blackhawks in the 2nd round of the 2016 entry draft. He has appeared in 294 NHL games during his career, notching 44 goals and adding 55 assists along with 267 penalty minutes. The 23 year old left wing has played 52 games with the New Jersey Devils this season, totalling 8+8=16 with 44 PIM.
7-6-2021 NJ Lucifer praises Sasha Pastujov
New Jersey Devils head coach NJ Lucifer has praised young right wing Sasha Pastujov in his latest interviews. Altough he seems very fond of this highly skilled youngster, he didn't want to comment on his team's strategy concerning the upcoming draft.

Sasha Pastujov has played 74 games with Swift Current Broncos this season, collecting 55+22=77 with 37 PIM.
