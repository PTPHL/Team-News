A native of Switzerland, Nico Hischier was originally drafted to the NHL by the New Jersey Devils in the 1st round of the 2017 entry draft. He has appeared in 227 NHL games during his career, notching 80 goals and adding 109 assists along with 53 penalty minutes. The 21 year old left wing has played 63 games with the New Jersey Devils this season, totalling 21+28=49 with 15 PIM.
