I just want to thank all the people that have supported me throughout my career. I couldn't have done this without them, was the comment released to the press after the game.

A native of Canada, Jonathan Marchessault, was originally signed as a free agent to the NHL. He has appeared in 287 NHL games during his career, notching 84 goals and adding 81 assists along with 131 penalty minutes. The 28 year old right wing has played 81 games with the New Jersey Devils this season, totalling 26+26=52 with 30 PIM.

1-4-2019 Player of the month: Auston Matthews (C)
NHL has awarded Auston Matthews (C) of New Jersey Devils as Player Of The Month He registered 10 goals and 10 assists for 20 points in 15 games last month. He also had 4 game winning goals.

A native of USA, Auston Matthews was originally drafted to the NHL by the Toronto Maple Leafs in the 1st round of the 2016 entry draft. He has appeared in 242 NHL games during his career, notching 103 goals and adding 102 assists along with 67 penalty minutes. The 21 year old center has played 78 games with the New Jersey Devils this season, totalling 34+39=73 with 24 PIM.
