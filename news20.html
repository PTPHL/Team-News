15-6-2019 New Jersey Devils trade
New Jersey Devils traded Blake Speers to Florida Panthers for Roberto Luongo and EDM's round 1 pick in 2019

A native of Canada, Roberto Luongo was originally drafted to the NHL by the New York Islanders in the 1st round of the 1997 entry draft. He has appeared in 982 NHL games during his career, booking 73 career shutouts and allowing 2359 goals. The 40 year old goaltender has not yet played for the Florida Panthers this season.

A native of Canada, Blake Speers was originally drafted to the NHL by the New Jersey Devils in the 3rd round of the 2015 entry draft. He has appeared in 7 NHL games during his career, notching 1 goals and adding 1 assists along with 0 penalty minutes. The 22 year old center has played 4 games with the New Jersey Devils this season, totalling 1+1=2 with 0 PIM. He has also played 78 games with the Binghamton Devils this season, totalling 48+71=119 with 8 PIM.

4-6-2019 Devin Shore frustrated
New Jersey Devils center Devin Shore feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a regular player.

A native of Canada, Devin Shore was originally drafted to the NHL by the Dallas Stars in the 2nd round of the 2012 entry draft. He has appeared in 85 NHL games during his career, notching 13 goals and adding 20 assists along with 14 penalty minutes. The 24 year old center has not yet played for the New Jersey Devils this season. He has played 41 games with the Binghamton Devils this season, totalling 17+17=34 with 12 PIM.
