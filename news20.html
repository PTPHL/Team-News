14-6-2019 NJ Auston Matthews praises Case McCarthy
New Jersey Devils head coach NJ Auston Matthews has praised young defenseman Case McCarthy in his latest interviews. Altough he seems very fond of this highly skilled youngster, he didn't want to comment on his team's strategy concerning the upcoming draft.

Case McCarthy has played 27 games with Boston University this season, collecting 8+15=23 with 11 PIM.
4-6-2019 Devin Shore frustrated
New Jersey Devils center Devin Shore feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a regular player.

A native of Canada, Devin Shore was originally drafted to the NHL by the Dallas Stars in the 2nd round of the 2012 entry draft. He has appeared in 85 NHL games during his career, notching 13 goals and adding 20 assists along with 14 penalty minutes. The 24 year old center has not yet played for the New Jersey Devils this season. He has played 41 games with the Binghamton Devils this season, totalling 17+17=34 with 12 PIM.
