27-6-2018 Connor Carrick frustrated
Toronto Maple Leafs defenseman Connor Carrick feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a regular player.

A native of USA, Connor Carrick was originally drafted to the NHL by the Washington Capitals in the 5th round of the 2012 entry draft. He has appeared in 202 NHL games during his career, notching 9 goals and adding 30 assists along with 129 penalty minutes. The 24 year old defenseman is getting ready for the upcoming season.
25-6-2018 Andreas Borgman frustrated
Toronto Maple Leafs defenseman Andreas Borgman feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Sweden, Andreas Borgman, was originally signed as a free agent to the NHL. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 23 year old defenseman is getting ready for the upcoming season.
24-6-2018 David Backes frustrated
Toronto Maple Leafs right wing David Backes feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of USA, David Backes was originally drafted to the NHL by the St. Louis Blues in the 2nd round of the 2003 entry draft. He has appeared in 883 NHL games during his career, notching 256 goals and adding 300 assists along with 1148 penalty minutes. The 34 year old right wing is getting ready for the upcoming season.
