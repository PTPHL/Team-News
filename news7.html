7-4-2020 Jordan Eberle reaches a milestone !
Jordan Eberle notched the 300th assist of his career in tonight's game against the Nashville Predators on a goal by Dominik Kubalik. Awesome, even more so because the game was televised, he commented during a post game radio interview..

A native of Canada, Jordan Eberle was originally drafted to the NHL by the Edmonton Oilers in the 1st round of the 2008 entry draft. He has appeared in 742 NHL games during his career, notching 228 goals and adding 300 assists along with 327 penalty minutes. The 29 year old right wing has played 72 games with the Chicago Blackhawks this season, totalling 14+20=34 with 54 PIM.
7-4-2020 Ryan Pulock reaches career high !
Ryan Pulock marked a new career high in goals with 8 tallies after scoring in tonights game against Nashville Predators on a feed by Robert Thomas. Hopefully one day this puck will end up in the Hall of Fame, he commented to the media.

A native of Canada, Ryan Pulock was originally drafted to the NHL by the New York Islanders in the 1st round of the 2013 entry draft. He has appeared in 221 NHL games during his career, notching 23 goals and adding 59 assists along with 162 penalty minutes. The 25 year old defenseman has played 81 games with the Chicago Blackhawks this season, totalling 8+21=29 with 84 PIM.
