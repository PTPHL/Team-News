22-6-2018 David Kampf frustrated
Chicago Blackhawks right wing David Kampf feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Czech, David Kampf, was originally signed as a free agent to the NHL. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 23 year old right wing is eagerly waiting to hit the ice once the season starts.

20-6-2018 Alpo Suhonen praises Jonatan Asplund
Chicago Blackhawks head coach Alpo Suhonen has praised young defenseman Jonatan Asplund in his latest interviews. Altough he seems very fond of this highly skilled youngster, he didn't want to comment on his team's strategy concerning the upcoming draft.

Jonatan Asplund has played 59 games with Djurgarden IF Stockholm this season, collecting 7+13=20 with 16 PIM.
