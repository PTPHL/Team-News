28-1-2019 Jonathan Toews reaches a milestone !
Jonathan Toews notched the 400th assist of his career in tonight's game against the Vancouver Canucks on a goal by Marian Hossa. It's a joy to be playing with these guys, they are the best teammates anyone could have, the happy player said in a hockey magazine..
