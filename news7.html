1-9-2019 Luc Snuggerud frustrated
Chicago Blackhawks defenseman Luc Snuggerud feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of USA, Luc Snuggerud was originally drafted to the NHL by the Chicago Blackhawks in the 5th round of the 2014 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 23 year old defenseman is getting ready for the upcoming season.
1-9-2019 Lucas Carlsson agrees on a contract.
Lucas Carlsson has signed a $575,000 per year, 2 year deal with the Chicago Blackhawks. Detailed terms of the deal were not announced.

A native of Sweden, Lucas Carlsson was originally drafted to the NHL by the Chicago Blackhawks in the 4th round of the 2016 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 22 year old defenseman is getting ready for the upcoming season.
