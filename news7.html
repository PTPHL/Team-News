4-10-2019 Jake Ryczek agrees on a contract.
Jake Ryczek has signed a $700,000 per year, 3 year deal with the Chicago Blackhawks. Detailed terms of the deal were not announced.

A native of USA, Jake Ryczek was originally drafted to the NHL by the Chicago Blackhawks in the 7th round of the 2016 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 21 year old defenseman looks to stay a member of the team for a long time.
3-10-2019 Carl Dahlstrom frustrated
Chicago Blackhawks defenseman Carl Dahlstrom feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Sweden, Carl Dahlstrom was originally drafted to the NHL by the Chicago Blackhawks in the 2nd round of the 2013 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 24 year old defenseman looks to stay a member of the team for a long time.
1-10-2019 Aidan Dudas sent back juniors
Chicago Blackhawks's Aidan Dudas did not make the team and was sent back for another year of junior hockey. The Chicago Blackhawks expect him to make another run at a roster spot next season.
30-9-2019 Team captain meets with Alex DeBrincat
Team captain Jonathan Toews called a short meeting with Alex DeBrincat after practice today to discuss some issues that have affected his play. I have to take the brunt of this, said Alex DeBrincat as he left the ice. My play has not been up to snuff and if I'm not contributing more, I have to work harder.
