10-1-2022 Dominik Kubalik reaches career high !
Dominik Kubalik marked a new career high in goals with 10 tallies after scoring in tonights game against Edmonton Oilers on a feed by Scott Laughton. I can't quite remember the last time when I was this happy, he said at the post-game press conference.

A native of Czech, Dominik Kubalik was originally drafted to the NHL by the Los Angeles Kings in the 7th round of the 2013 entry draft. He has appeared in 173 NHL games during his career, notching 27 goals and adding 36 assists along with 171 penalty minutes. The 26 year old left wing has played 43 games with the Chicago Blackhawks this season, totalling 10+16=26 with 65 PIM.
