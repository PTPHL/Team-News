14-12-2018 Player of the week: Nikita Gusev (L)
NHL has awarded Nikita Gusev (L) of Chicago Blackhawks as Player Of The Week He registered 3 goals and 4 assists for 7 points in 3 games last week. He also had 1 game winning goal.

A native of Russia, Nikita Gusev was originally drafted to the NHL by the Tampa Bay Lightning in the 7th round of the 2012 entry draft. He has appeared in 75 NHL games during his career, notching 12 goals and adding 21 assists along with 29 penalty minutes. The 26 year old left wing has played 31 games with the Chicago Blackhawks this season, totalling 8+13=21 with 11 PIM.
