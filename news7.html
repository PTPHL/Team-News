28-6-2019 Matt Iacopelli frustrated
Chicago Blackhawks right wing Matt Iacopelli feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of USA, Matt Iacopelli was originally drafted to the NHL by the Chicago Blackhawks in the 3rd round of the 2014 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 25 year old right wing is waiting for a good start this season.
