5-4-2019 Samuel Kurker frustrated

4-4-2019 Alex Pietrangelo reaches a milestone !
Alex Pietrangelo notched the 300th assist of his career in tonight's game against the Minnesota Wild on a goal by Joe Pavelski. It's always nice to give a good pass..., was all he could say after the game..

A native of Canada, Alex Pietrangelo was originally drafted to the NHL by the St. Louis Blues in the 1st round of the 2008 entry draft. He has appeared in 699 NHL games during his career, notching 77 goals and adding 300 assists along with 286 penalty minutes. The 29 year old defenseman has played 79 games with the Chicago Blackhawks this season, totalling 4+29=33 with 71 PIM.
