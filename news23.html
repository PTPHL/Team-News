31-5-2023 Klim Kostin frustrated
Vegas Golden Knights left wing Klim Kostin feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Russia, Klim Kostin was originally drafted to the NHL by the St. Louis Blues in the 1st round of the 2017 entry draft. The 24 year old left wing has played 29 games with the Vegas Golden Knights this season, totalling 9+5=14 with 27 PIM.
