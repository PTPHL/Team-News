5-4-2020 Charlie Coyle reaches career high !
Charlie Coyle marked a new career high in points with 57 after scoring in tonights game against San Jose Sharks on a feed by Tyler Seguin. I don't know why exactly, but the ice just seems to be more wide open for me this year, the player said in an interview.

A native of USA, Charlie Coyle was originally drafted to the NHL by the San Jose Sharks in the 1st round of the 2010 entry draft. He has appeared in 602 NHL games during his career, notching 145 goals and adding 168 assists along with 241 penalty minutes. The 28 year old right wing has played 80 games with the Vegas Golden Knights this season, totalling 32+25=57 with 31 PIM.
