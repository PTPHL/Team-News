31-8-2018 Jacob De La Rose frustrated
Vegas Golden Knights left wing Jacob De La Rose feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Sweden, Jacob De La Rose was originally drafted to the NHL by the Montreal Canadiens in the 2nd round of the 2013 entry draft. He has appeared in 91 NHL games during his career, notching 6 goals and adding 8 assists along with 39 penalty minutes. The 23 year old left wing is eagerly waiting to hit the ice once the season starts.
