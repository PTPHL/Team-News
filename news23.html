26-6-2018 Teemu Pulkkinen frustrated
Vegas Golden Knights right wing Teemu Pulkkinen feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a regular player.

A native of Finland, Teemu Pulkkinen was originally drafted to the NHL by the Detroit Red Wings in the 4th round of the 2010 entry draft. He has appeared in 128 NHL games during his career, notching 27 goals and adding 30 assists along with 46 penalty minutes. The 26 year old right wing is expecting a good season for the team.
