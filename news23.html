1-9-2022 Samuel Dove-Mcfalls frustrated
Vegas Golden Knights left wing Samuel Dove-Mcfalls feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.
