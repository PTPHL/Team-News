8-5-2019 Maxime Lagacé frustrated
Vegas Golden Knights goaltender Maxime Lagacé feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a regular player.

A native of Canada, Maxime Lagacé, was originally signed as a free agent to the NHL. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 26 year old goaltender has not yet played for the Vegas Golden Knights this season.
