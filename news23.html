8-3-2020 Joel Eriksson Ek reaches career high !
Joel Eriksson Ek marked a new career high in points with 37 after scoring in tonights game against Vancouver Canucks on a feed by Brent Seabrook. Yes, it's nice to break records but that's not the idea of this game, were his words after the game.

A native of Sweden, Joel Eriksson Ek was originally drafted to the NHL by the Minnesota Wild in the 1st round of the 2015 entry draft. He has appeared in 164 NHL games during his career, notching 30 goals and adding 50 assists along with 183 penalty minutes. The 23 year old center has played 67 games with the Vegas Golden Knights this season, totalling 15+22=37 with 81 PIM.

6-3-2020 Anders Lee reaches a milestone !
Anders Lee scored the 300th point of his career in tonight's game against the Nashville Predators on a goal by Eeli Tolvanen. All of them are special, but I'm going to remember this one the most, he said with a wink..

A native of USA, Anders Lee was originally drafted to the NHL by the New York Islanders in the 6th round of the 2009 entry draft. He has appeared in 491 NHL games during his career, notching 144 goals and adding 156 assists along with 389 penalty minutes. The 29 year old right wing has played 66 games with the Vegas Golden Knights this season, totalling 18+31=49 with 80 PIM.
