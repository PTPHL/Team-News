28-6-2019 Brendan Gaunce frustrated
Vancouver Canucks left wing Brendan Gaunce feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a regular player.

A native of Canada, Brendan Gaunce was originally drafted to the NHL by the Vancouver Canucks in the 1st round of the 2012 entry draft. He has appeared in 241 NHL games during his career, notching 18 goals and adding 23 assists along with 89 penalty minutes. The 25 year old left wing is looking forward for the start of the season.
