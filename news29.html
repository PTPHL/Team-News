28-6-2018 Tyler Bertuzzi frustrated
Vancouver Canucks left wing Tyler Bertuzzi feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Tyler Bertuzzi was originally drafted to the NHL by the Detroit Red Wings in the 2nd round of the 2013 entry draft. He has appeared in 50 NHL games during his career, notching 6 goals and adding 5 assists along with 28 penalty minutes. The 23 year old left wing is getting ready for the upcoming season.
