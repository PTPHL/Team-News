5-4-2020 Michael Chaput frustrated
Vancouver Canucks center Michael Chaput feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a regular player.

A native of Canada, Michael Chaput was originally drafted to the NHL by the Philadelphia Flyers in the 3rd round of the 2010 entry draft. He has appeared in 298 NHL games during his career, notching 24 goals and adding 37 assists along with 114 penalty minutes. The 27 year old center has played 77 games with the Vancouver Canucks this season, totalling 10+13=23 with 31 PIM.
