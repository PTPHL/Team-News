11-6-2021 Vancouver Canucks trade
Vancouver Canucks traded Jonathan Toews to New York Rangers for Pavel Buchnevich and NYR's round 1 pick in 2021

A native of Russia, Pavel Buchnevich was originally drafted to the NHL by the New York Rangers in the 3rd round of the 2013 entry draft. He has appeared in 365 NHL games during his career, notching 79 goals and adding 100 assists along with 246 penalty minutes. The 26 year old right wing has played 78 games with the New York Rangers this season, totalling 19+18=37 with 117 PIM.

A native of Canada, Jonathan Toews was originally drafted to the NHL by the Chicago Blackhawks in the 1st round of the 2006 entry draft. He has appeared in 1042 NHL games during his career, notching 360 goals and adding 465 assists along with 622 penalty minutes. The 33 year old center has played 82 games with the Vancouver Canucks this season, totalling 25+29=54 with 38 PIM.
