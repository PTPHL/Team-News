15-9-2018 Brendan Gaunce frustrated
Vancouver Canucks left wing Brendan Gaunce feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a regular player.

A native of Canada, Brendan Gaunce was originally drafted to the NHL by the Vancouver Canucks in the 1st round of the 2012 entry draft. He has appeared in 159 NHL games during his career, notching 8 goals and adding 12 assists along with 61 penalty minutes. The 24 year old left wing has not yet played for the Vancouver Canucks this season.
