1-5-2020 Rookie of the month: Kirby Dach (L)
NHL has awarded Kirby Dach (L) of San Jose Sharks as Rookie Of The Month He registered 4 goals and 8 assists for 12 points in 12 games last month.

A native of Canada, Kirby Dach was originally drafted to the NHL by the San Jose Sharks in the 1st round of the 2019 entry draft. He has appeared in 11 NHL games during his career, notching 3 goals and adding 7 assists along with 12 penalty minutes. The 19 year old left wing has played 11 games with the San Jose Sharks this season, totalling 3+7=10 with 12 PIM. He has also played 71 games with the San Jose Barracuda this season, totalling 37+32=69 with 16 PIM.
