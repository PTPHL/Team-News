5-9-2020 Mike Robinson frustrated
San Jose Sharks goaltender Mike Robinson feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of USA, Mike Robinson was originally drafted to the NHL by the San Jose Sharks in the 3rd round of the 2015 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 23 year old goaltender is eagerly waiting to hit the ice once the season starts.
