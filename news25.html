25-6-2019 Barclay Goodrow frustrated
San Jose Sharks left wing Barclay Goodrow feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a regular player.

A native of Canada, Barclay Goodrow, was originally signed as a free agent to the NHL. He has appeared in 238 NHL games during his career, notching 18 goals and adding 35 assists along with 113 penalty minutes. The 26 year old left wing is eagerly waiting to hit the ice once the season starts.

21-6-2019 Alex Tuch frustrated
San Jose Sharks center Alex Tuch feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of USA, Alex Tuch was originally drafted to the NHL by the Minnesota Wild in the 1st round of the 2014 entry draft. He has appeared in 149 NHL games during his career, notching 31 goals and adding 40 assists along with 36 penalty minutes. The 23 year old center is expecting a good season for the team.
