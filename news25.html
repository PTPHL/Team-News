10-5-2019 Anthony Duclair frustrated
San Jose Sharks left wing Anthony Duclair feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Anthony Duclair was originally drafted to the NHL by the New York Rangers in the 3rd round of the 2013 entry draft. He has appeared in 323 NHL games during his career, notching 57 goals and adding 79 assists along with 205 penalty minutes. The 23 year old left wing has played 82 games with the San Jose Sharks this season, totalling 13+21=34 with 78 PIM.
