7-6-2020 SJ Lebas Sucks praises Jonatan Asplund
San Jose Sharks head coach SJ Lebas Sucks has praised young defenseman Jonatan Asplund in his latest interviews. Altough he seems very fond of this highly skilled youngster, he didn't want to comment on his team's strategy concerning the upcoming draft.

Jonatan Asplund has played 60 games with Djurgarden IF Stockholm this season, collecting 9+14=23 with 49 PIM.
