7-4-2020 Stefan Noesen reaches career high !
Stefan Noesen marked a new career high in assists with 29 assists after assisting in tonights game against Dallas Stars on a goal by Henri Jokiharju. I'm just happy to contribute, the player said in an interview.

A native of USA, Stefan Noesen was originally drafted to the NHL by the Ottawa Senators in the 1st round of the 2011 entry draft. He has appeared in 266 NHL games during his career, notching 56 goals and adding 65 assists along with 93 penalty minutes. The 27 year old left wing has played 80 games with the San Jose Sharks this season, totalling 24+29=53 with 31 PIM.

5-4-2020 Timo Meier reaches career high !
Timo Meier marked a new career high in points with 54 after scoring in tonights game against Vegas Golden Knights on a feed by Alex Tuch. When you come to play, good things happen, the happy player commented.

A native of Switzerland, Timo Meier was originally drafted to the NHL by the San Jose Sharks in the 1st round of the 2015 entry draft. He has appeared in 277 NHL games during his career, notching 64 goals and adding 94 assists along with 181 penalty minutes. The 23 year old right wing has played 79 games with the San Jose Sharks this season, totalling 24+30=54 with 63 PIM.

Tomas Hertl (SJS) suffered abdomen injury during the game in an incident with Brent Seabrook (VGK). He is expected to be sidelined for a couple of days. I should be back in the lineup soon, it doesn't feel that serious, the injured player commented.
