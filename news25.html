A native of Czech, Tomas Hertl was originally drafted to the NHL by the San Jose Sharks in the 1st round of the 2012 entry draft. He has appeared in 390 NHL games during his career, notching 96 goals and adding 129 assists along with 128 penalty minutes. The 25 year old left wing has played 68 games with the San Jose Sharks this season, totalling 20+31=51 with 44 PIM.
