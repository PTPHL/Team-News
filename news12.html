Jack Roslovic marked a new career high in points with 51 after scoring in tonights game against Minnesota Wild on a feed by Ryan Nugent-Hopkins. I'm not out here to set records, but it's always nice to get the points, he said at the post-game press conference.

A native of USA, Jack Roslovic was originally drafted to the NHL by the Columbus Blue Jackets in the 1st round of the 2015 entry draft.
