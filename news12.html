12-1-2019 Darnell Nurse frustrated
Edmonton Oilers defenseman Darnell Nurse feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Darnell Nurse was originally drafted to the NHL by the Edmonton Oilers in the 1st round of the 2013 entry draft. He has appeared in 243 NHL games during his career, notching 14 goals and adding 39 assists along with 147 penalty minutes. The 23 year old defenseman has played 46 games with the Edmonton Oilers this season, totalling 1+7=8 with 30 PIM.
