7-12-2021 Luke Kunin frustrated
Edmonton Oilers right wing Luke Kunin feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of USA, Luke Kunin was originally drafted to the NHL by the Minnesota Wild in the 1st round of the 2016 entry draft. He has appeared in 324 NHL games during his career, notching 61 goals and adding 67 assists along with 200 penalty minutes. The 24 year old right wing has played 30 games with the Edmonton Oilers this season, totalling 9+7=16 with 49 PIM.
