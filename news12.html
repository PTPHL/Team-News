10-1-2022 Kristian Tanus reaches career high !
Kristian Tanus marked a new career high in goals with 11 tallies after scoring in tonights game against Chicago Blackhawks on a feed by Joel Farabee. We played some good, fundamental hockey tonight. When you do that the goals always follow, he said at the post-game press conference.

A native of Finland, Kristian Tanus was originally drafted to the NHL by the Ottawa Senators in the 1st round of the 2018 entry draft. He has appeared in 114 NHL games during his career, notching 21 goals and adding 26 assists along with 128 penalty minutes. The 21 year old right wing has played 44 games with the Edmonton Oilers this season, totalling 11+12=23 with 58 PIM.
