12-6-2020 Ryan Mantha frustrated
Edmonton Oilers defenseman Ryan Mantha feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of USA, Ryan Mantha was originally drafted to the NHL by the New York Rangers in the 4th round of the 2014 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 23 year old defenseman has not yet played for the Edmonton Oilers this season. He has played 82 games with the Bakersfield Condors this season, totalling 2+11=13 with 30 PIM.
