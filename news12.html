14-12-2018 Valeri Nichushkin frustrated
Edmonton Oilers right wing Valeri Nichushkin feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Russia, Valeri Nichushkin was originally drafted to the NHL by the Dallas Stars in the 1st round of the 2013 entry draft. He has appeared in 264 NHL games during his career, notching 41 goals and adding 68 assists along with 72 penalty minutes. The 23 year old right wing has played 33 games with the Edmonton Oilers this season, totalling 7+6=13 with 12 PIM.
