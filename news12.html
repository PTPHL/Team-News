13-6-2019 Nick Schmaltz frustrated
Edmonton Oilers center Nick Schmaltz feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of USA, Nick Schmaltz was originally drafted to the NHL by the Chicago Blackhawks in the 1st round of the 2014 entry draft.
