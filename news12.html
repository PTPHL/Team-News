14-12-2021 Bo Horvat frustrated
Edmonton Oilers center Bo Horvat feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a franchise player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Bo Horvat was originally drafted to the NHL by the Vancouver Canucks in the 1st round of the 2013 entry draft. He has appeared in 564 NHL games during his career, notching 138 goals and adding 185 assists along with 216 penalty minutes. The 26 year old center has played 33 games with the Edmonton Oilers this season, totalling 10+15=25 with 50 PIM.
