12-5-2023 MacKenzie Entwistle frustrated
Philadelphia Flyers right wing MacKenzie Entwistle feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, MacKenzie Entwistle was originally drafted to the NHL by the Vegas Golden Knights in the 3rd round of the 2017 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 23 year old right wing has not yet played for the Philadelphia Flyers this season.
