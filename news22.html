Junior players have finished their seasons in the juniors and are now back with their respective teams.
15-6-2019 Radel Fazleyev frustrated
Philadelphia Flyers left wing Radel Fazleyev feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Russia, Radel Fazleyev was originally drafted to the NHL by the Philadelphia Flyers in the 6th round of the 2014 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 23 year old left wing has not yet played for the Philadelphia Flyers this season. He has played 82 games with the Lehigh Valley Phantoms this season, totalling 10+9=19 with 8 PIM.
13-6-2019 PHI Fred Shero praises Filip Prikryl
Philadelphia Flyers head coach PHI Fred Shero has praised young center Filip Prikryl in his latest interviews. Altough he seems very fond of this highly skilled youngster, he didn't want to comment on his team's strategy concerning the upcoming draft.

Filip Prikryl has played 73 games with Erie Otters this season, collecting 44+29=73 with 69 PIM.
