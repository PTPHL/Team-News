10-3-2019 Claude Giroux reaches a milestone !
Claude Giroux scored the 700th point of his career in tonight's game against the New York Rangers on a goal by Shayne Gostisbehere. That's one for the books, the player said in an interview..

A native of Canada, Claude Giroux was originally drafted to the NHL by the Philadelphia Flyers in the 1st round of the 2006 entry draft. He has appeared in 806 NHL games during his career, notching 235 goals and adding 465 assists along with 394 penalty minutes. The 31 year old center has played 68 games with the Philadelphia Flyers this season, totalling 25+33=58 with 42 PIM.
