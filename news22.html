8-11-2021 Nolan Patrick reaches a milestone !
Nolan Patrick scored the 100th goal of his career in tonight's game against the Pittsburgh Penguins on a feed by Travis Sanheim. I just feel so happy now, this comes at a good time for me, the happy player commented.

A native of Canada, Nolan Patrick was originally drafted to the NHL by the Philadelphia Flyers in the 1st round of the 2017 entry draft. He has appeared in 342 NHL games during his career, notching 100 goals and adding 125 assists along with 228 penalty minutes. The 23 year old center has played 14 games with the Philadelphia Flyers this season, totalling 4+7=11 with 25 PIM.
8-11-2021 Ryan Getzlaf reaches a milestone !
Ryan Getzlaf notched the 700th assist of his career in tonight's game against the Pittsburgh Penguins on a goal by Mark Stone. We were playing our game tonight and I just happened to be in the right place at the right time, he said after the game in the dressing room..

A native of Canada, Ryan Getzlaf was originally drafted to the NHL by the Anaheim Ducks in the 1st round of the 2003 entry draft. He has appeared in 1205 NHL games during his career, notching 324 goals and adding 700 assists along with 914 penalty minutes. The 36 year old right wing has played 14 games with the Philadelphia Flyers this season, totalling 4+9=13 with 4 PIM.
8-11-2021 MacKenzie Entwistle injured.
MacKenzie Entwistle of Lehigh Valley Phantoms suffered injury during tonights minorleague game against the Wilkes-Barre/Scranton Penguins. He is expected to be sidelined for almost a month . I wish I could contribute, but I'll be out of action for awhile. Hopefully some of the others can step up their play a bit in the meantime, the injured player commented.
