2-12-2018 Andrew Shaw reaches a milestone !
Andrew Shaw scored the 100th goal of his career in tonight's game against the Ottawa Senators on a feed by Sean Couturier. It's not so big but I'm still very happy about it, he commented to the media.

A native of Canada, Andrew Shaw was originally drafted to the NHL by the Chicago Blackhawks in the 5th round of the 2011 entry draft. He has appeared in 497 NHL games during his career, notching 100 goals and adding 110 assists along with 483 penalty minutes. The 27 year old right wing has played 25 games with the Philadelphia Flyers this season, totalling 5+5=10 with 40 PIM.
