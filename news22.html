24-2-2020 Claude Giroux reaches a milestone !
Claude Giroux notched the 500th assist of his career in tonight's game against the Tampa Bay Lightning on a goal by Mark Stone. The pass just found the right guy, what more can I say, he commented to the media..

A native of Canada, Claude Giroux was originally drafted to the NHL by the Philadelphia Flyers in the 1st round of the 2006 entry draft. He has appeared in 881 NHL games during his career, notching 264 goals and adding 500 assists along with 438 penalty minutes. The 32 year old center has played 61 games with the Philadelphia Flyers this season, totalling 23+31=54 with 40 PIM.
