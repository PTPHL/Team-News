29-6-2019 Nicolas Aubé-Kubel frustrated
Philadelphia Flyers right wing Nicolas Aubé-Kubel feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Nicolas Aubé-Kubel was originally drafted to the NHL by the Philadelphia Flyers in the 2nd round of the 2014 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 23 year old right wing is expecting a good season for the team.
