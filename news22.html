29-11-2018 Sean Couturier reaches a milestone !
Sean Couturier scored the 100th goal of his career in tonight's game against the Nashville Predators on a feed by Wayne Simmonds. As long as the team wins I'm happy. The personal records don't mean anything to me if they aren't a part of winning the Cup, was his only statement before going to the dressing room.

A native of Canada, Sean Couturier was originally drafted to the NHL by the Philadelphia Flyers in the 1st round of the 2011 entry draft. He has appeared in 521 NHL games during his career, notching 100 goals and adding 159 assists along with 190 penalty minutes. The 25 year old center has played 23 games with the Philadelphia Flyers this season, totalling 6+7=13 with 4 PIM.
