8-3-2020 Andrew Shaw reaches career high !
Andrew Shaw marked a new career high in points with 43 after scoring in tonights game against Calgary Flames on a feed by Nolan Patrick. In a game like this, it was nice to set that milestone, was his only comment for the media.

A native of Canada, Andrew Shaw was originally drafted to the NHL by the Chicago Blackhawks in the 5th round of the 2011 entry draft. He has appeared in 621 NHL games during his career, notching 138 goals and adding 147 assists along with 620 penalty minutes. The 28 year old right wing has played 67 games with the Philadelphia Flyers this season, totalling 21+22=43 with 100 PIM.
