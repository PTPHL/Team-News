14-1-2022 Pascal Laberge frustrated
Philadelphia Flyers center Pascal Laberge feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Pascal Laberge was originally drafted to the NHL by the Philadelphia Flyers in the 2nd round of the 2016 entry draft. He has appeared in 23 NHL games during his career, notching 3 goals and adding 7 assists along with 27 penalty minutes. The 23 year old center has not yet played for the Philadelphia Flyers this season. He has played 44 games with the Lehigh Valley Phantoms this season, totalling 8+32=40 with 40 PIM.
