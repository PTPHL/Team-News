29-6-2018 Victor Ejdsell frustrated
Philadelphia Flyers center Victor Ejdsell feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Sweden, Victor Ejdsell, was originally signed as a free agent to the NHL. He has appeared in 13 NHL games during his career, notching 2 goals and adding 5 assists along with 2 penalty minutes. The 23 year old center is looking forward for the start of the season.
