17-3-2020 Ryan Getzlaf reaches a milestone !
Ryan Getzlaf scored the 300th goal of his career in tonight's game against the Florida Panthers on a feed by Corey Perry. I just feel so happy now, this comes at a good time for me, he pointed out to the gathered press corps.

A native of Canada, Ryan Getzlaf was originally drafted to the NHL by the Anaheim Ducks in the 1st round of the 2003 entry draft. He has appeared in 1099 NHL games during his career, notching 300 goals and adding 658 assists along with 848 penalty minutes. The 34 year old center has played 72 games with the Ottawa Senators this season, totalling 18+24=42 with 32 PIM.
