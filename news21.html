7-6-2020 Alex Lintuniemi frustrated
Ottawa Senators defenseman Alex Lintuniemi feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Finland, Alex Lintuniemi was originally drafted to the NHL by the Los Angeles Kings in the 2nd round of the 2014 entry draft. He has appeared in 60 NHL games during his career, notching 3 goals and adding 10 assists along with 33 penalty minutes. The 24 year old defenseman has played 60 games with the Ottawa Senators this season, totalling 3+10=13 with 33 PIM. He has also played 23 games with the Belleville Senators this season, totalling 3+7=10 with 12 PIM.
