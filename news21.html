30-9-2020 Ottawa Senators trade

A native of Russia, Nail Yakupov was originally drafted to the NHL by the Edmonton Oilers in the 1st round of the 2012 entry draft. He has appeared in 329 NHL games during his career, notching 61 goals and adding 75 assists along with 138 penalty minutes. The 26 year old left wing is getting ready for the upcoming season.

A native of Canada, AJ Greer was originally drafted to the NHL by the Colorado Avalanche in the 2nd round of the 2015 entry draft. He has appeared in 60 NHL games during his career, notching 4 goals and adding 2 assists along with 62 penalty minutes. The 23 year old right wing is looking forward for the start of the season.

A native of Finland, Markus Granlund was originally drafted to the NHL by the Calgary Flames in the 2nd round of the 2011 entry draft. He has appeared in 379 NHL games during his career, notching 64 goals and adding 61 assists along with 193 penalty minutes. The 27 year old left wing is waiting for a good start this season.

A native of Canada, Ryan Getzlaf was originally drafted to the NHL by the Anaheim Ducks in the 1st round of the 2003 entry draft. He has appeared in 1109 NHL games during his career, notching 300 goals and adding 660 assists along with 852 penalty minutes. The 35 year old center is expecting a good season for the team.
