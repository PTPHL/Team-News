2-11-2022 Nino Niederreiter reaches a milestone !
Nino Niederreiter scored the 500th point of his career in tonight's game against the New York Rangers on a goal by Ville Pokka. I don't know why exactly, but the ice just seems to be more wide open for me this year, our record breaker told a Hockey Night in Canada reporter..

A native of Switzerland, Nino Niederreiter was originally drafted to the NHL by the New York Islanders in the 1st round of the 2010 entry draft. He has appeared in 796 NHL games during his career, notching 217 goals and adding 283 assists along with 563 penalty minutes. The 30 year old right wing has played 11 games with the Ottawa Senators this season, totalling 4+4=8 with 2 PIM.
