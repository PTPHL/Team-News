7-1-2022 Boris Katchouk frustrated
Ottawa Senators right wing Boris Katchouk feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Boris Katchouk was originally drafted to the NHL by the Tampa Bay Lightning in the 2nd round of the 2016 entry draft. He has appeared in 168 NHL games during his career, notching 24 goals and adding 23 assists along with 301 penalty minutes. The 23 year old right wing has played 41 games with the Ottawa Senators this season, totalling 12+11=23 with 57 PIM.
