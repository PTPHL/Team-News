6-3-2020 Shane Eiserman frustrated
Ottawa Senators left wing Shane Eiserman feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of USA, Shane Eiserman was originally drafted to the NHL by the Ottawa Senators in the 4th round of the 2014 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 24 year old left wing has not yet played for the Ottawa Senators this season. He has played 35 games with the Belleville Senators this season, totalling 3+3=6 with 18 PIM.
