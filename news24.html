5-3-2020 Dominik Masin frustrated
Pittsburgh Penguins defenseman Dominik Masin feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Czech, Dominik Masin was originally drafted to the NHL by the Tampa Bay Lightning in the 2nd round of the 2014 entry draft. He has appeared in 144 NHL games during his career, notching 3 goals and adding 32 assists along with 115 penalty minutes. The 24 year old defenseman has played 31 games with the Pittsburgh Penguins this season, totalling 1+4=5 with 44 PIM. He has also played 32 games with the Wilkes-Barre/Scranton Penguins this season, totalling 1+10=11 with 10 PIM.

3-3-2020 Tyson Jost reaches career high !
Tyson Jost marked a new career high in goals with 19 tallies after scoring in tonights game against Tampa Bay Lightning on a feed by Taylor Raddysh. I just try and give it my best out there. This one was special though, he commented after giving the puck to a fan.

A native of Canada, Tyson Jost was originally drafted to the NHL by the Colorado Avalanche in the 1st round of the 2016 entry draft. He has appeared in 235 NHL games during his career, notching 55 goals and adding 62 assists along with 100 penalty minutes. The 21 year old right wing has played 65 games with the Pittsburgh Penguins this season, totalling 19+11=30 with 42 PIM.
