6-12-2018 Tyler Seguin reaches a milestone !
Tyler Seguin notched the 300th assist of his career in tonight's game against the Boston Bruins on a goal by Phil Kessel. Awesome, even more so because the game was televised, the happy player said in a hockey magazine..

A native of Canada, Tyler Seguin was originally drafted to the NHL by the Boston Bruins in the 1st round of the 2010 entry draft. He has appeared in 617 NHL games during his career, notching 229 goals and adding 300 assists along with 178 penalty minutes. The 26 year old center has played 26 games with the Pittsburgh Penguins this season, totalling 7+13=20 with 14 PIM.
