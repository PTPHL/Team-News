29-6-2019 Jamie Oleksiak frustrated
Pittsburgh Penguins defenseman Jamie Oleksiak feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a regular player.

A native of Canada, Jamie Oleksiak was originally drafted to the NHL by the Dallas Stars in the 1st round of the 2011 entry draft. He has appeared in 283 NHL games during his career, notching 10 goals and adding 38 assists along with 142 penalty minutes. The 26 year old defenseman is looking forward for the start of the season.
