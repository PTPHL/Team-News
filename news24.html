2-9-2020 Zach Aston-Reese frustrated
Pittsburgh Penguins right wing Zach Aston-Reese feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of USA, Zach Aston-Reese, was originally signed as a free agent to the NHL. He has appeared in 78 NHL games during his career, notching 9 goals and adding 11 assists along with 122 penalty minutes. The 26 year old right wing is expecting a good season for the team.
