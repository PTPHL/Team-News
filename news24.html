15-6-2020 Zach Aston-Reese frustrated
Pittsburgh Penguins right wing Zach Aston-Reese feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of USA, Zach Aston-Reese, was originally signed as a free agent to the NHL. He has appeared in 78 NHL games during his career, notching 9 goals and adding 11 assists along with 122 penalty minutes. The 25 year old right wing has not yet played for the Pittsburgh Penguins this season. He has played 82 games with the Wilkes-Barre/Scranton Penguins this season, totalling 56+18=74 with 10 PIM.
