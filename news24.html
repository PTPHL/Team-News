24-6-2018 Joshua Morrissey frustrated
Pittsburgh Penguins defenseman Joshua Morrissey feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Joshua Morrissey was originally drafted to the NHL by the Columbus Blue Jackets in the 1st round of the 2013 entry draft. He has appeared in 168 NHL games during his career, notching 10 goals and adding 38 assists along with 84 penalty minutes. The 23 year old defenseman is expecting a good season for the team.
