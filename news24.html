7-6-2020 PIT Head Coach praises Cole Perfetti
Pittsburgh Penguins head coach PIT Head Coach has praised young center Cole Perfetti in his latest interviews. Altough he seems very fond of this highly skilled youngster, he didn't want to comment on his team's strategy concerning the upcoming draft.

Cole Perfetti has played 27 games with Minn. St-Mankato this season, collecting 22+10=32 with 44 PIM.
