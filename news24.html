30-9-2019 Dysin Mayo frustrated
Pittsburgh Penguins defenseman Dysin Mayo feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Dysin Mayo was originally drafted to the NHL by the Vegas Golden Knights in the 5th round of the 2014 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 23 year old defenseman is expecting a good season for the team.
30-9-2019 Zach Aston-Reese frustrated
Pittsburgh Penguins right wing Zach Aston-Reese feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of USA, Zach Aston-Reese, was originally signed as a free agent to the NHL. He has appeared in 78 NHL games during his career, notching 9 goals and adding 11 assists along with 122 penalty minutes. The 25 year old right wing is waiting for a good start this season.
