28-2-2020 Nino Niederreiter reaches career high !
Nino Niederreiter marked a new career high in assists with 37 assists after assisting in tonights game against Vegas Golden Knights on a goal by Ryan Murray. Awesome, even more so because the game was televised, the happy player said in a hockey magazine.

A native of Switzerland, Nino Niederreiter was originally drafted to the NHL by the New York Islanders in the 1st round of the 2010 entry draft. He has appeared in 602 NHL games during his career, notching 145 goals and adding 181 assists along with 307 penalty minutes. The 27 year old right wing has played 61 games with the Winnipeg Jets this season, totalling 18+37=55 with 16 PIM.
