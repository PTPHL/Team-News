10-5-2021 Patrik Laine frustrated
Winnipeg Jets right wing Patrik Laine feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a franchise player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Finland, Patrik Laine was originally drafted to the NHL by the Columbus Blue Jackets in the 1st round of the 2016 entry draft. He has appeared in 401 NHL games during his career, notching 166 goals and adding 193 assists along with 144 penalty minutes. The 23 year old right wing has played 82 games with the Winnipeg Jets this season, totalling 27+52=79 with 14 PIM.
