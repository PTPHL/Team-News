11-6-2023 Winnipeg Jets celebrate Stanley Cup
The Stanley Cup Champions Winnipeg Jets were celebrated in their hometown with grand festivities today. The Stanley Cup was driven through the town in a parade and the fans were chanting the team's name every time captain Mark Scheifele lifted the Cup above his head.

The mayor of the town greeted the players on a stage with the media surrounding them as masses of loyal Winnipeg Jets fans chanted the names of Mark Scheifele and Juuse Saros.

Today is our day, no one will ever take this away from us or from you guys, spoke team captain Mark Scheifele to the fans.

9-6-2023 Nicholas Caamano frustrated
