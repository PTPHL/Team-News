28-6-2019 Jack Glover frustrated
Winnipeg Jets defenseman Jack Glover feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of USA, Jack Glover was originally drafted to the NHL by the Columbus Blue Jackets in the 3rd round of the 2014 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 23 year old defenseman is getting ready for the upcoming season.

25-6-2019 William Nylander frustrated
Winnipeg Jets right wing William Nylander feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Sweden, William Nylander was originally drafted to the NHL by the Toronto Maple Leafs in the 1st round of the 2014 entry draft. He has appeared in 267 NHL games during his career, notching 68 goals and adding 114 assists along with 142 penalty minutes. The 23 year old right wing is eagerly waiting to hit the ice once the season starts.
