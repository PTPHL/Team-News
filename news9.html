8-5-2020 Denis Malgin frustrated
Winnipeg Jets center Denis Malgin feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Switzerland, Denis Malgin was originally drafted to the NHL by the Florida Panthers in the 4th round of the 2015 entry draft. He has appeared in 145 NHL games during his career, notching 15 goals and adding 18 assists along with 58 penalty minutes. The 23 year old center has not yet played for the Winnipeg Jets this season. He has played 80 games with the Manitoba Moose this season, totalling 25+47=72 with 14 PIM.

NHL has awarded William Nylander (C) of Winnipeg Jets as Player Of The Week He registered 4 goals and 0 assists for 4 points in 2 games last week. He also had 1 game winning goal.
