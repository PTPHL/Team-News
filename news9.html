5-4-2019 Nikolaj Ehlers reaches career high !
Nikolaj Ehlers marked a new career high in goals with 29 tallies after scoring in tonights game against Minnesota Wild on a feed by Patrik Laine. The coach gave me a chance to play, and I'm just trying to show him he made the right decision, he said at the post-game press conference.

A native of La-la-land, Nikolaj Ehlers was originally drafted to the NHL by the Columbus Blue Jackets in the 1st round of the 2014 entry draft. He has appeared in 316 NHL games during his career, notching 97 goals and adding 123 assists along with 127 penalty minutes. The 23 year old right wing has played 80 games with the Winnipeg Jets this season, totalling 29+29=58 with 26 PIM.
