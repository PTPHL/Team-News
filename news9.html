5-5-2023 Player of the week: Mark Scheifele (C)
NHL has awarded Mark Scheifele (C) of Winnipeg Jets as Player Of The Week He registered 5 goals and 2 assists for 7 points in 4 games last week. He also had 2 game winning goals.

A native of Canada, Mark Scheifele was originally drafted to the NHL by the Columbus Blue Jackets in the 1st round of the 2011 entry draft. He has appeared in 798 NHL games during his career, notching 248 goals and adding 335 assists along with 435 penalty minutes. The 30 year old center has played 82 games with the Winnipeg Jets this season, totalling 24+33=57 with 20 PIM.
