23-1-2022 Patrik Laine reaches a milestone !
Patrik Laine scored the 400th point of his career in tonight's game against the Buffalo Sabres on a goal by Mark Scheifele. I just want to thank all the people that have supported me throughout my career. I couldn't have done this without them, the player said in an interview..

A native of Finland, Patrik Laine was originally drafted to the NHL by the Columbus Blue Jackets in the 1st round of the 2016 entry draft. He has appeared in 449 NHL games during his career, notching 180 goals and adding 220 assists along with 148 penalty minutes. The 23 year old right wing has played 48 games with the Winnipeg Jets this season, totalling 14+27=41 with 4 PIM.
