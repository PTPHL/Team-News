3-4-2020 Nino Niederreiter reaches career high !

A native of Switzerland, Nino Niederreiter was originally drafted to the NHL by the New York Islanders in the 1st round of the 2010 entry draft. He has appeared in 617 NHL games during his career, notching 148 goals and adding 185 assists along with 323 penalty minutes. The 27 year old right wing has played 76 games with the Winnipeg Jets this season, totalling 21+41=62 with 32 PIM.
