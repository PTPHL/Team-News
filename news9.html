13-1-2019 Colin White reaches career high !
Colin White marked a new career high in goals with 14 tallies after scoring in tonights game against Vegas Golden Knights on a feed by Patrik Laine. I just feel so happy now, this comes at a good time for me, he said at the post-game press conference.

A native of USA, Colin White was originally drafted to the NHL by the Ottawa Senators in the 1st round of the 2015 entry draft. He has appeared in 101 NHL games during his career, notching 27 goals and adding 50 assists along with 50 penalty minutes. The 21 year old center has played 45 games with the Winnipeg Jets this season, totalling 14+20=34 with 12 PIM.

11-1-2019 Brandon Carlo reaches career high !
Brandon Carlo marked a new career high in assists with 11 assists after assisting in tonights game against Minnesota Wild on a goal by Patrik Laine. I'm happy about it but that's it, gotta think other things now, he read from a prepared speech.

A native of USA, Brandon Carlo was originally drafted to the NHL by the Boston Bruins in the 2nd round of the 2015 entry draft. He has appeared in 126 NHL games during his career, notching 9 goals and adding 21 assists along with 71 penalty minutes. The 22 year old defenseman has played 44 games with the Winnipeg Jets this season, totalling 3+11=14 with 12 PIM.
