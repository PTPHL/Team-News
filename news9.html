20-12-2018 Antoine Vermette reaches a milestone !
Antoine Vermette notched the 300th assist of his career in tonight's game against the Toronto Maple Leafs on a goal by Eric Staal. I'm just happy to contribute, were his words after the game..

A native of Canada, Antoine Vermette was originally drafted to the NHL by the Ottawa Senators in the 2nd round of the 2000 entry draft. He has appeared in 1099 NHL games during his career, notching 236 goals and adding 300 assists along with 650 penalty minutes. The 36 year old center has played 35 games with the Winnipeg Jets this season, totalling 6+7=13 with 18 PIM.
