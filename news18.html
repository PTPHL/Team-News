26-2-2020 Brett Howden reaches career high !
Brett Howden marked a new career high in points with 29 after scoring in tonights game against Toronto Maple Leafs on a feed by Kevin Shattenkirk. I hope this will get me in Don Cherry's good books, he said after the game in the dressing room.

A native of Canada, Brett Howden was originally drafted to the NHL by the Tampa Bay Lightning in the 1st round of the 2016 entry draft. He has appeared in 143 NHL games during his career, notching 24 goals and adding 33 assists along with 100 penalty minutes. The 21 year old center has played 61 games with the New York Rangers this season, totalling 12+17=29 with 47 PIM.
