10-3-2019 Mika Zibanejad reaches career high !
Mika Zibanejad marked a new career high in points with 52 after scoring in tonights game against Philadelphia Flyers on a feed by Ryan Spooner. It is just part of my job, our record breaker told a Hockey Night in Canada reporter.

A native of Sweden, Mika Zibanejad was originally drafted to the NHL by the Ottawa Senators in the 1st round of the 2011 entry draft. He has appeared in 486 NHL games during his career, notching 117 goals and adding 162 assists along with 142 penalty minutes. The 25 year old center has played 67 games with the New York Rangers this season, totalling 23+29=52 with 16 PIM.
