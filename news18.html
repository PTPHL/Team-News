27-6-2018 Jimmy Vesey frustrated
New York Rangers left wing Jimmy Vesey feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a regular player.

A native of USA, Jimmy Vesey was originally drafted to the NHL by the Nashville Predators in the 3rd round of the 2012 entry draft. He has appeared in 162 NHL games during his career, notching 26 goals and adding 31 assists along with 68 penalty minutes. The 25 year old left wing is looking forward for the start of the season.
