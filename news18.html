11-11-2018 Mika Zibanejad reaches a milestone !
Mika Zibanejad scored the 100th goal of his career in tonight's game against the Montreal Canadiens on a feed by Michael Dal Colle. You can't worry about the personal achievements - it's the W's that count, was his only statement before going to the dressing room.

A native of Sweden, Mika Zibanejad was originally drafted to the NHL by the Ottawa Senators in the 1st round of the 2011 entry draft. He has appeared in 434 NHL games during his career, notching 100 goals and adding 141 assists along with 134 penalty minutes. The 25 year old center has played 15 games with the New York Rangers this season, totalling 6+8=14 with 8 PIM.
