27-6-2019 Chris Bigras frustrated
New York Rangers defenseman Chris Bigras feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a regular player.

A native of Canada, Chris Bigras was originally drafted to the NHL by the Colorado Avalanche in the 2nd round of the 2013 entry draft. He has appeared in 58 NHL games during his career, notching 3 goals and adding 6 assists along with 34 penalty minutes. The 24 year old defenseman is expecting a good season for the team.
