8-4-2019 Andreas Athanasiou reaches career high !
Andreas Athanasiou marked a new career high in goals with 19 tallies after scoring in tonights game against Calgary Flames on a feed by Filip Zadina. I just feel so happy now, this comes at a good time for me, he said at the post-game press conference.

A native of Canada, Andreas Athanasiou was originally drafted to the NHL by the Detroit Red Wings in the 4th round of the 2012 entry draft. He has appeared in 253 NHL games during his career, notching 57 goals and adding 56 assists along with 110 penalty minutes. The 24 year old left wing has played 81 games with the Colorado Avalanche this season, totalling 19+23=42 with 43 PIM.
