1-12-2021 Player of the month: Filip Zadina (L)
NHL has awarded Filip Zadina (L) of Colorado Avalanche as Player Of The Month He registered 9 goals and 9 assists for 18 points in 14 games last month. He also had 2 game winning goals.

A native of Czech, Filip Zadina was originally drafted to the NHL by the Colorado Avalanche in the 1st round of the 2018 entry draft. He has appeared in 159 NHL games during his career, notching 46 goals and adding 58 assists along with 84 penalty minutes. The 22 year old left wing has played 26 games with the Colorado Avalanche this season, totalling 12+13=25 with 6 PIM.
