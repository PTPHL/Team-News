7-11-2021 Andreas Athanasiou reaches a milestone !
Andreas Athanasiou scored the 100th goal of his career in tonight's game against the Minnesota Wild on a feed by Ty Smith. As long as the team wins I'm happy. The personal records don't mean anything to me if they aren't a part of winning the Cup, were his words after the game.

A native of Canada, Andreas Athanasiou was originally drafted to the NHL by the Detroit Red Wings in the 4th round of the 2012 entry draft. He has appeared in 432 NHL games during his career, notching 100 goals and adding 109 assists along with 157 penalty minutes. The 27 year old left wing has played 14 games with the Colorado Avalanche this season, totalling 7+8=15 with 17 PIM.
