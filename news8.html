11-5-2019 COL Head Coach praises Alex Turcotte
Colorado Avalanche head coach COL Head Coach has praised young center Alex Turcotte in his latest interviews. Altough he seems very fond of this highly skilled youngster, he didn't want to comment on his team's strategy concerning the upcoming draft.

Alex Turcotte has played 29 games with Wisconsin this season, collecting 22+13=35 with 9 PIM.
