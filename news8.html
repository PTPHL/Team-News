2-4-2022 Teemu Pulkkinen reaches career high !
Teemu Pulkkinen marked a new career high in assists with 22 assists after assisting in tonights game against San Jose Sharks on a goal by Alex Newhook. Sometimes the puck just bounces your way..., he said to the press.

A native of Finland, Teemu Pulkkinen was originally drafted to the NHL by the Detroit Red Wings in the 4th round of the 2010 entry draft. He has appeared in 364 NHL games during his career, notching 68 goals and adding 78 assists along with 132 penalty minutes. The 30 year old right wing has played 73 games with the Colorado Avalanche this season, totalling 25+22=47 with 27 PIM.
