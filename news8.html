7-6-2022 Rhett Holland frustrated
Colorado Avalanche defenseman Rhett Holland feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Rhett Holland was originally drafted to the NHL by the Vegas Golden Knights in the 4th round of the 2012 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 28 year old defenseman has not yet played for the Colorado Avalanche this season. He has played 82 games with the Colorado Eagles this season, totalling 3+3=6 with 10 PIM.
6-6-2022 Max Gildon frustrated
Colorado Avalanche defenseman Max Gildon feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of USA, Max Gildon was originally drafted to the NHL by the Florida Panthers in the 3rd round of the 2017 entry draft. He has appeared in 330 NHL games during his career, notching 11 goals and adding 54 assists along with 291 penalty minutes. The 23 year old defenseman has played 84 games with the Colorado Avalanche this season, totalling 2+19=21 with 76 PIM.
