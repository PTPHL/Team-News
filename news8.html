3-3-2020 Andreas Athanasiou reaches career high !
Andreas Athanasiou marked a new career high in assists with 24 assists after assisting in tonights game against Buffalo Sabres on a goal by Markus Granlund. It's a joy to be playing with these guys, they are the best teammates anyone could have, he said to the press.

A native of Canada, Andreas Athanasiou was originally drafted to the NHL by the Detroit Red Wings in the 4th round of the 2012 entry draft. He has appeared in 317 NHL games during his career, notching 71 goals and adding 80 assists along with 118 penalty minutes. The 25 year old left wing has played 63 games with the Colorado Avalanche this season, totalling 14+24=38 with 8 PIM.
