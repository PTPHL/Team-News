10-2-2019 P.K. Subban reaches a milestone !
P.K. Subban notched the 300th assist of his career in tonight's game against the Winnipeg Jets on a goal by Ryan Hartman. I've been fortunate enough to play with many great players and I could not have done it without them, he said at the post-game press conference..

A native of Canada, P.K. Subban was originally drafted to the NHL by the Montreal Canadiens in the 2nd round of the 2007 entry draft. He has appeared in 636 NHL games during his career, notching 91 goals and adding 300 assists along with 650 penalty minutes. The 29 year old defenseman has played 54 games with the Nashville Predators this season, totalling 7+20=27 with 24 PIM.
