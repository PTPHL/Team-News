24-6-2018 Niko Ojamaki frustrated
Nashville Predators right wing Niko Ojamaki feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Finland, Niko Ojamaki, was originally signed as a free agent to the NHL. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 23 year old right wing is looking forward for the start of the season.
23-6-2018 Juuse Saros frustrated
Nashville Predators goaltender Juuse Saros feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Finland, Juuse Saros was originally drafted to the NHL by the Nashville Predators in the 4th round of the 2013 entry draft. He has appeared in 34 NHL games during his career, booking 1 career shutouts and allowing 88 goals. The 23 year old goaltender is waiting for a good start this season.
