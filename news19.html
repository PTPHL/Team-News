6-3-2020 Kasperi Kapanen reaches career high !
Kasperi Kapanen marked a new career high in goals with 16 tallies after scoring in tonights game against Vegas Golden Knights on a feed by Kevin Fiala. I just try and give it my best out there. This one was special though, he said before boarding the teams chartered jet.

A native of Finland, Kasperi Kapanen was originally drafted to the NHL by the Pittsburgh Penguins in the 1st round of the 2014 entry draft. He has appeared in 248 NHL games during his career, notching 39 goals and adding 38 assists along with 72 penalty minutes. The 23 year old left wing has played 67 games with the Nashville Predators this season, totalling 16+13=29 with 12 PIM.
