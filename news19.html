17-11-2018 Roman Josi reaches a milestone !
Roman Josi scored the 300th point of his career in tonight's game against the Florida Panthers on a feed by James Neal. I'm not out here to set records, but it's always nice to get the points, he commented during a post game radio interview.

A native of Switzerland, Roman Josi was originally drafted to the NHL by the Nashville Predators in the 2nd round of the 2008 entry draft. He has appeared in 507 NHL games during his career, notching 75 goals and adding 225 assists along with 157 penalty minutes. The 28 year old defenseman has played 19 games with the Nashville Predators this season, totalling 3+10=13 with 4 PIM.
