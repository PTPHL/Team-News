8-11-2021 Viktor Arvidsson reaches a milestone !
Viktor Arvidsson scored the 300th point of his career in tonight's game against the San Jose Sharks on a goal by Kevin Fiala. I've seen my idols break records and now I am doing the same, were his words after the game..

A native of Sweden, Viktor Arvidsson was originally drafted to the NHL by the Nashville Predators in the 4th round of the 2014 entry draft. He has appeared in 470 NHL games during his career, notching 137 goals and adding 163 assists along with 290 penalty minutes. The 28 year old right wing has played 14 games with the Nashville Predators this season, totalling 6+8=14 with 8 PIM.
