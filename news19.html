Nashville Predators center Nolan Stevens feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Nolan Stevens was originally drafted to the NHL by the St. Louis Blues in the 5th round of the 2016 entry draft. He has appeared in 145 NHL games during his career, notching 10 goals and adding 18 assists along with 104 penalty minutes. The 26 year old center is looking forward for the start of the season.
