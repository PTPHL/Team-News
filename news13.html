20-10-2018 Brendan Gallagher reaches a milestone !
Brendan Gallagher scored the 100th goal of his career in tonight's game against the Colorado Avalanche on a feed by Dan Girardi. We played some good, fundamental hockey tonight. When you do that the goals always follow, the happy player said in a hockey magazine.

A native of Canada, Brendan Gallagher was originally drafted to the NHL by the Montreal Canadiens in the 5th round of the 2010 entry draft. He has appeared in 410 NHL games during his career, notching 100 goals and adding 121 assists along with 304 penalty minutes. The 26 year old right wing has played 4 games with the Florida Panthers this season, totalling 2+0=2 with 2 PIM.
