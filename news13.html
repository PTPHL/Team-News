1-11-2021 Patrik Berglund reaches a milestone !
Patrik Berglund scored the 400th point of his career in tonight's game against the New York Rangers on a feed by Nikita Zaitsev. I just want to thank all the people that have supported me throughout my career. I couldn't have done this without them, he said after the game.

A native of Sweden, Patrik Berglund was originally drafted to the NHL by the St. Louis Blues in the 1st round of the 2006 entry draft. He has appeared in 975 NHL games during his career, notching 193 goals and adding 207 assists along with 282 penalty minutes. The 33 year old left wing has played 10 games with the Florida Panthers this season, totalling 3+4=7 with 2 PIM.
