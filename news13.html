4-2-2022 Blake Speers frustrated
Florida Panthers center Blake Speers feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Blake Speers was originally drafted to the NHL by the New Jersey Devils in the 3rd round of the 2015 entry draft.
