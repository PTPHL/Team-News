31-3-2021 Blake Speers frustrated
Florida Panthers center Blake Speers feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Blake Speers was originally drafted to the NHL by the New Jersey Devils in the 3rd round of the 2015 entry draft. He has appeared in 167 NHL games during his career, notching 16 goals and adding 28 assists along with 195 penalty minutes. The 24 year old center has played 78 games with the Florida Panthers this season, totalling 6+11=17 with 127 PIM.
