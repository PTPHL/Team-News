7-3-2022 Adam Gaudette reaches career high !
Adam Gaudette marked a new career high in points with 29 after assisting in tonights game against San Jose Sharks on a goal by Brendan Gallagher. Yes, it does feel great, our record breaker told a Hockey Night in Canada reporter..

A native of USA, Adam Gaudette was originally drafted to the NHL by the Vancouver Canucks in the 5th round of the 2015 entry draft. He has appeared in 326 NHL games during his career, notching 41 goals and adding 57 assists along with 213 penalty minutes. The 25 year old right wing has played 67 games with the Florida Panthers this season, totalling 16+13=29 with 43 PIM.
