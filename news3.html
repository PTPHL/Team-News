4-2-2023 Nikita Zadorov reaches career high !
Nikita Zadorov marked a new career high in assists with 27 assists after assisting in tonights game against Florida Panthers on a goal by James van Riemsdyk. It's a joy to be playing with these guys, they are the best teammates anyone could have, the happy player said in a hockey magazine.

A native of Russia, Nikita Zadorov was originally drafted to the NHL by the Buffalo Sabres in the 1st round of the 2013 entry draft. He has appeared in 608 NHL games during his career, notching 29 goals and adding 171 assists along with 482 penalty minutes. The 27 year old defenseman has played 54 games with the Boston Bruins this season, totalling 3+27=30 with 17 PIM.
