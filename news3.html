18-2-2019 David Pastrnak reaches a milestone !
David Pastrnak scored the 100th goal of his career in tonight's game against the Carolina Hurricanes on a feed by Blake Wheeler. I wouldn't have been able to do this without my great teammates, the happy player said in a hockey magazine.

A native of Czech, David Pastrnak was originally drafted to the NHL by the Boston Bruins in the 1st round of the 2014 entry draft. He has appeared in 312 NHL games during his career, notching 100 goals and adding 116 assists along with 151 penalty minutes. The 22 year old right wing has played 58 games with the Boston Bruins this season, totalling 19+21=40 with 24 PIM.
