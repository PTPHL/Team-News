7-3-2019 Linus Arnesson frustrated
Boston Bruins defenseman Linus Arnesson feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Sweden, Linus Arnesson was originally drafted to the NHL by the Boston Bruins in the 2nd round of the 2013 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 24 year old defenseman has not yet played for the Boston Bruins this season. He has played 67 games with the Providence Bruins this season, totalling 0+8=8 with 12 PIM.

6-3-2019 Yanni Gourde reaches career high !
Yanni Gourde marked a new career high in goals with 7 tallies after scoring in tonights game against Montreal Canadiens on a feed by Troy Stecher. I'm just happy to be playing the game, he pointed out to the gathered press corps.

A native of Canada, Yanni Gourde, was originally signed as a free agent to the NHL. He has appeared in 170 NHL games during his career, notching 17 goals and adding 24 assists along with 118 penalty minutes. The 27 year old left wing has played 66 games with the Boston Bruins this season, totalling 7+5=12 with 32 PIM.
