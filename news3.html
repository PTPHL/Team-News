27-6-2019 Jared McCann frustrated
Boston Bruins left wing Jared McCann feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Jared McCann was originally drafted to the NHL by the Vancouver Canucks in the 1st round of the 2014 entry draft. He has appeared in 230 NHL games during his career, notching 25 goals and adding 39 assists along with 90 penalty minutes. The 23 year old left wing is waiting for a good start this season.

20-6-2019 Emil Johansson frustrated
Boston Bruins defenseman Emil Johansson feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Sweden, Emil Johansson was originally drafted to the NHL by the Boston Bruins in the 7th round of the 2014 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 23 year old defenseman has not yet played for the Boston Bruins this season. He has played 82 games with the Providence Bruins this season, totalling 5+3=8 with 10 PIM.
