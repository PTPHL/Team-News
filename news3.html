8-6-2023 Alexis Gravel frustrated
Boston Bruins goaltender Alexis Gravel feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Alexis Gravel was originally drafted to the NHL by the Boston Bruins in the 2nd round of the 2018 entry draft. He has appeared in 98 NHL games during his career, booking 2 career shutouts and allowing 314 goals. The 23 year old goaltender has played 18 games with the Boston Bruins this season, allowing 49 goals on 390 while booking 0 shutouts.
5-6-2023 BOS Claudio Sanchez praises Alexis Binner
Boston Bruins head coach BOS Claudio Sanchez has praised young defenseman Alexis Binner in his latest interviews. Altough he seems very fond of this highly skilled youngster, he didn't want to comment on his team's strategy concerning the upcoming draft.

Alexis Binner has played 57 games with Frölunda Göteborg this season, collecting 6+13=19 with 25 PIM.
