5-11-2018 David Krejci reaches a milestone !
David Krejci scored the 600th point of his career in tonight's game against the Toronto Maple Leafs on a goal by Blake Wheeler. Yes, it does feel great, were his words after the game..

A native of Czech, David Krejci was originally drafted to the NHL by the Boston Bruins in the 2nd round of the 2004 entry draft. He has appeared in 801 NHL games during his career, notching 186 goals and adding 414 assists along with 306 penalty minutes. The 32 year old center has played 14 games with the Boston Bruins this season, totalling 7+8=15 with 6 PIM.
