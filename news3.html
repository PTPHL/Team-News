18-6-2021 Players return from juniors
Junior players have finished their seasons in the juniors and are now back with their respective teams.
13-6-2021 Joona Koppanen frustrated
Boston Bruins right wing Joona Koppanen feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Finland, Joona Koppanen was originally drafted to the NHL by the Boston Bruins in the 5th round of the 2016 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 23 year old right wing has not yet played for the Boston Bruins this season. He has played 82 games with the Providence Bruins this season, totalling 16+15=31 with 6 PIM.
12-6-2021 BOS Claudio Sanchez praises Sasha Pastujov
Boston Bruins head coach BOS Claudio Sanchez has praised young right wing Sasha Pastujov in his latest interviews. Altough he seems very fond of this highly skilled youngster, he didn't want to comment on his team's strategy concerning the upcoming draft.

Sasha Pastujov has played 74 games with Swift Current Broncos this season, collecting 55+22=77 with 37 PIM.
4-6-2021 BOS Claudio Sanchez praises Eero Niemi
