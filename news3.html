7-4-2021 James van Riemsdyk reaches career high !
James van Riemsdyk marked a new career high in assists with 34 assists after assisting in tonights game against New Jersey Devils on a goal by Zachary Senyshyn. Whatever I can do to help the team is okay by me. As long as we get the 2 points that's the main thing, he read from a prepared speech.

A native of USA, James van Riemsdyk was originally drafted to the NHL by the Philadelphia Flyers in the 1st round of the 2007 entry draft. He has appeared in 852 NHL games during his career, notching 251 goals and adding 286 assists along with 552 penalty minutes. The 31 year old left wing has played 81 games with the Boston Bruins this season, totalling 23+34=57 with 86 PIM.
