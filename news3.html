A native of Canada, Zachary Senyshyn was originally drafted to the NHL by the Boston Bruins in the 1st round of the 2015 entry draft. He has appeared in 174 NHL games during his career, notching 34 goals and adding 25 assists along with 45 penalty minutes. The 23 year old right wing has not yet played for the Boston Bruins this season.
