6-1-2023 Oskar Steen frustrated
Boston Bruins right wing Oskar Steen feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Sweden, Oskar Steen was originally drafted to the NHL by the Boston Bruins in the 6th round of the 2016 entry draft. He has appeared in 106 NHL games during his career, notching 13 goals and adding 23 assists along with 140 penalty minutes. The 24 year old right wing has played 41 games with the Boston Bruins this season, totalling 3+4=7 with 40 PIM.
