6-4-2020 Will Butcher reaches career high !
Will Butcher marked a new career high in points with 39 after assisting in tonights game against New Jersey Devils on a goal by Kirill Kaprizov. I just want to thank this great organization for presenting me with the opportunity to play in the NHL, he said to the press..

A native of USA, Will Butcher was originally drafted to the NHL by the Colorado Avalanche in the 5th round of the 2013 entry draft. He has appeared in 244 NHL games during his career, notching 17 goals and adding 87 assists along with 115 penalty minutes. The 25 year old defenseman has played 80 games with the Boston Bruins this season, totalling 5+34=39 with 43 PIM.
