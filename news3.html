Charlie McAvoy marked a new career high in assists with 26 assists after assisting in tonights game against Columbus Blue Jackets on a goal by Kaapo Kakko. Hey, I'm just happy it went in the net. Wins are more important than assist records for us right now, he said after viewing the play on the TV studio monitor.

A native of USA, Charlie McAvoy was originally drafted to the NHL by the Boston Bruins in the 1st round of the 2016 entry draft. He has appeared in 226 NHL games during his career, notching 15 goals and adding 74 assists along with 154 penalty minutes. The 22 year old defenseman has played 68 games with the Boston Bruins this season, totalling 5+26=31 with 64 PIM.
