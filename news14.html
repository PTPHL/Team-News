4-12-2018 Sven Andrighetto frustrated
Los Angeles Kings right wing Sven Andrighetto feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a regular player.

A native of Switzerland, Sven Andrighetto was originally drafted to the NHL by the Montreal Canadiens in the 3rd round of the 2013 entry draft. He has appeared in 160 NHL games during his career, notching 26 goals and adding 40 assists along with 70 penalty minutes. The 25 year old right wing has not yet played for the Los Angeles Kings this season.
