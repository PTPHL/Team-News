5-6-2019 Alex Lintuniemi frustrated
Los Angeles Kings defenseman Alex Lintuniemi feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Finland, Alex Lintuniemi was originally drafted to the NHL by the Los Angeles Kings in the 2nd round of the 2014 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 23 year old defenseman has not yet played for the Los Angeles Kings this season. He has played 82 games with the Ontario Reign this season, totalling 2+28=30 with 12 PIM.
3-6-2019 Tobias Rieder frustrated
Los Angeles Kings left wing Tobias Rieder feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Germany, Tobias Rieder was originally drafted to the NHL by the Edmonton Oilers in the 4th round of the 2011 entry draft. He has appeared in 398 NHL games during his career, notching 97 goals and adding 129 assists along with 72 penalty minutes. The 26 year old left wing has played 82 games with the Los Angeles Kings this season, totalling 24+39=63 with 16 PIM.
