22-6-2019 Matt Buckles frustrated
Los Angeles Kings center Matt Buckles feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Matt Buckles was originally drafted to the NHL by the Florida Panthers in the 4th round of the 2013 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 24 year old center is getting ready for the upcoming season.
