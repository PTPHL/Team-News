18-6-2020 Players return from juniors
Junior players have finished their seasons in the juniors and are now back with their respective teams.
15-6-2020 Jake Virtanen frustrated
Los Angeles Kings right wing Jake Virtanen feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Jake Virtanen was originally drafted to the NHL by the Vancouver Canucks in the 1st round of the 2014 entry draft. He has appeared in 286 NHL games during his career, notching 50 goals and adding 64 assists along with 308 penalty minutes. The 23 year old right wing has played 82 games with the Los Angeles Kings this season, totalling 13+18=31 with 67 PIM.
14-6-2020 LA Head Coach praises Lorenzo Crawford
Los Angeles Kings head coach LA Head Coach has praised young right wing Lorenzo Crawford in his latest interviews. Altough he seems very fond of this highly skilled youngster, he didn't want to comment on his team's strategy concerning the upcoming draft.

Lorenzo Crawford has played 30 games with Union this season, collecting 24+8=32 with 5 PIM.
