8-1-2023 Reilly Smith reaches a milestone !
Reilly Smith scored the 200th goal of his career in tonight's game against the New Jersey Devils on a feed by Kaedan Korczak. I'm just happy to be playing the game, he said to the press.

A native of Canada, Reilly Smith was originally drafted to the NHL by the Dallas Stars in the 3rd round of the 2009 entry draft. He has appeared in 813 NHL games during his career, notching 200 goals and adding 282 assists along with 465 penalty minutes. The 31 year old left wing has played 38 games with the St. Louis Blues this season, totalling 15+18=33 with 47 PIM.
