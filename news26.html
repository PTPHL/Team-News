8-10-2021 Patrick Kane reaches a milestone !
Patrick Kane notched the 600th assist of his career in tonight's game against the Vegas Golden Knights on a goal by Connor Murphy. It's a joy to be playing with these guys, they are the best teammates anyone could have, he said after viewing the play on the TV studio monitor..

A native of USA, Patrick Kane was originally drafted to the NHL by the Chicago Blackhawks in the 1st round of the 2007 entry draft. He has appeared in 1036 NHL games during his career, notching 419 goals and adding 600 assists along with 449 penalty minutes. The 32 year old right wing has played 2 games with the St. Louis Blues this season, totalling 1+2=3 with 0 PIM.
