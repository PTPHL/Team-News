9-6-2021 Thomas Chabot frustrated
St. Louis Blues defenseman Thomas Chabot feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a franchise player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Thomas Chabot was originally drafted to the NHL by the Ottawa Senators in the 1st round of the 2015 entry draft. He has appeared in 313 NHL games during his career, notching 34 goals and adding 138 assists along with 183 penalty minutes. The 24 year old defenseman has played 66 games with the St. Louis Blues this season, totalling 5+28=33 with 53 PIM.
