10-12-2018 Evander Kane reaches a milestone !
Evander Kane scored the 200th goal of his career in tonight's game against the Chicago Blackhawks on a feed by Patrick Kane. The main thing is we played like a team out there, and didn't quit. We've got to keep that up if we're going to challenge this year, he said outside the teams dressing room.

A native of Canada, Evander Kane was originally drafted to the NHL by the Columbus Blue Jackets in the 1st round of the 2009 entry draft. He has appeared in 603 NHL games during his career, notching 200 goals and adding 183 assists along with 662 penalty minutes. The 27 year old left wing has played 26 games with the St. Louis Blues this season, totalling 13+11=24 with 23 PIM.
10-12-2018 Ondrej Palat reaches a milestone !
Ondrej Palat scored the 100th goal of his career in tonight's game against the Chicago Blackhawks on a feed by Curtis Lazar. I'm just happy to be playing the game, he said outside the teams dressing room.
