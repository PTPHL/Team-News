15-12-2022 Kaedan Korczak reaches career high !
Kaedan Korczak marked a new career high in assists with 14 assists after assisting in tonights game against Columbus Blue Jackets on a goal by Dylan Cozens. Tonight things went my way and what can I say, i got the record, he said as he packed up his gear.

A native of Canada, Kaedan Korczak was originally drafted to the NHL by the St. Louis Blues in the 2nd round of the 2019 entry draft. He has appeared in 66 NHL games during his career, notching 5 goals and adding 27 assists along with 59 penalty minutes. The 21 year old defenseman has played 27 games with the St. Louis Blues this season, totalling 5+14=19 with 4 PIM.
