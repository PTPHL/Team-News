14-3-2021 Oskar Sundqvist reaches career high !
Oskar Sundqvist marked a new career high in goals with 11 tallies after scoring in tonights game against Calgary Flames on a feed by Reilly Smith. We have a good team this year, I'm just happy I can help out, the player said in an interview.

A native of Sweden, Oskar Sundqvist was originally drafted to the NHL by the Pittsburgh Penguins in the 3rd round of the 2012 entry draft. He has appeared in 308 NHL games during his career, notching 34 goals and adding 56 assists along with 75 penalty minutes. The 26 year old center has played 68 games with the St. Louis Blues this season, totalling 11+13=24 with 21 PIM.
