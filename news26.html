20-6-2019 Kyle Wood frustrated
St. Louis Blues defenseman Kyle Wood feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Kyle Wood was originally drafted to the NHL by the Colorado Avalanche in the 3rd round of the 2014 entry draft. He has appeared in 103 NHL games during his career, notching 6 goals and adding 13 assists along with 12 penalty minutes. The 23 year old defenseman has played 77 games with the St. Louis Blues this season, totalling 4+10=14 with 2 PIM.
