3-4-2021 Derek Forbort reaches career high !
Derek Forbort marked a new career high in assists with 17 assists after assisting in tonights game against Detroit Red Wings on a goal by Ondrej Palat. It's a joy to be playing with these guys, they are the best teammates anyone could have, the happy player commented.

A native of USA, Derek Forbort was originally drafted to the NHL by the Los Angeles Kings in the 1st round of the 2010 entry draft. He has appeared in 420 NHL games during his career, notching 11 goals and adding 64 assists along with 120 penalty minutes. The 29 year old defenseman has played 78 games with the St. Louis Blues this season, totalling 2+17=19 with 29 PIM.

1-4-2021 Reilly Smith reaches a milestone !

A native of Canada, Reilly Smith was originally drafted to the NHL by the Dallas Stars in the 3rd round of the 2009 entry draft. He has appeared in 688 NHL games during his career, notching 166 goals and adding 234 assists along with 299 penalty minutes.
