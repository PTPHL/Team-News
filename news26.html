14-6-2019 STL Head Coach praises Cam York
St. Louis Blues head coach STL Head Coach has praised young defenseman Cam York in his latest interviews. Altough he seems very fond of this highly skilled youngster, he didn't want to comment on his team's strategy concerning the upcoming draft.

Cam York has played 29 games with Boston College this season, collecting 9+17=26 with 14 PIM.
6-6-2019 Conor Garland frustrated
St. Louis Blues right wing Conor Garland feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of USA, Conor Garland was originally drafted to the NHL by the Vegas Golden Knights in the 5th round of the 2015 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 23 year old right wing has not yet played for the St. Louis Blues this season. He has played 74 games with the San Antonio Rampage this season, totalling 4+7=11 with 16 PIM.
