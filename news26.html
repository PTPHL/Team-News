1-6-2022 David Noël frustrated
St. Louis Blues defenseman David Noël feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, David Noël was originally drafted to the NHL by the St. Louis Blues in the 5th round of the 2017 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 23 year old defenseman has not yet played for the St. Louis Blues this season. He has played 82 games with the San Antonio Rampage this season, totalling 2+11=13 with 8 PIM.
