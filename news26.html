9-11-2022 Evander Kane reaches a milestone !
Evander Kane notched the 300th assist of his career in tonight's game against the Colorado Avalanche on a goal by Patrick Kane. For me this is dream come true, he commented and was all smiles..

A native of Canada, Evander Kane was originally drafted to the NHL by the Columbus Blue Jackets in the 1st round of the 2009 entry draft. He has appeared in 919 NHL games during his career, notching 284 goals and adding 300 assists along with 925 penalty minutes. The 31 year old center has played 14 games with the St. Louis Blues this season, totalling 5+5=10 with 4 PIM.
