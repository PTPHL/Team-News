6-3-2023 William Karlsson reaches a milestone !
William Karlsson scored the 400th point of his career in tonight's game against the Minnesota Wild on a goal by Jonathan Marchessault. The puck has been bouncing for me lately, he commented and was all smiles..

A native of Sweden, William Karlsson was originally drafted to the NHL by the Anaheim Ducks in the 2nd round of the 2011 entry draft. He has appeared in 663 NHL games during his career, notching 185 goals and adding 215 assists along with 303 penalty minutes. The 30 year old left wing has played 66 games with the St. Louis Blues this season, totalling 22+31=53 with 36 PIM.
