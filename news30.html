6-12-2021 Nicklas Bäckström reaches a milestone !
Nicklas Bäckström scored the 1000th point of his career in tonight's game against the Columbus Blue Jackets on a feed by Alex Ovechkin. That's one for the books, he said while signing autographs for fans.

A native of Sweden, Nicklas Bäckström was originally drafted to the NHL by the Washington Capitals in the 1st round of the 2006 entry draft. He has appeared in 1087 NHL games during his career, notching 311 goals and adding 689 assists along with 689 penalty minutes. The 34 year old center has played 25 games with the Washington Capitals this season, totalling 9+9=18 with 31 PIM.
