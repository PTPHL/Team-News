7-1-2019 Alex Ovechkin reaches a milestone !
Alex Ovechkin scored the 600th goal of his career in tonight's game against the Chicago Blackhawks on a feed by T.J. Oshie. With line mates like I have, it makes it pretty easy to score goals, he said outside the teams dressing room.

A native of Russia, Alex Ovechkin was originally drafted to the NHL by the Washington Capitals in the 1st round of the 2004 entry draft. He has appeared in 1042 NHL games during his career, notching 600 goals and adding 528 assists along with 717 penalty minutes. The 33 year old left wing has played 39 games with the Washington Capitals this season, totalling 13+16=29 with 36 PIM.
