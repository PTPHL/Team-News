3-11-2018 Nicklas Bäckström reaches a milestone !
Nicklas Bäckström scored the 800th point of his career in tonight's game against the New York Islanders on a feed by Alex Ovechkin. I just want to thank this great organization for presenting me with the opportunity to play in the NHL, the happy player said in a hockey magazine.

A native of Sweden, Nicklas Bäckström was originally drafted to the NHL by the Washington Capitals in the 1st round of the 2006 entry draft. He has appeared in 827 NHL games during his career, notching 220 goals and adding 580 assists along with 444 penalty minutes. The 30 year old center has played 11 games with the Washington Capitals this season, totalling 3+4=7 with 6 PIM.
