5-6-2022 Michal Kempny frustrated
Washington Capitals defenseman Michal Kempny feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a regular player.

A native of Czech, Michal Kempny, was originally signed as a free agent to the NHL. He has appeared in 460 NHL games during his career, notching 18 goals and adding 78 assists along with 236 penalty minutes. The 31 year old defenseman has played 82 games with the Washington Capitals this season, totalling 3+21=24 with 76 PIM.
