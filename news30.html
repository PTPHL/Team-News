14-6-2020 Max Domi frustrated
Washington Capitals left wing Max Domi feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Max Domi was originally drafted to the NHL by the Vegas Golden Knights in the 1st round of the 2013 entry draft. He has appeared in 386 NHL games during his career, notching 93 goals and adding 167 assists along with 281 penalty minutes. The 25 year old left wing has played 82 games with the Washington Capitals this season, totalling 22+30=52 with 45 PIM.
