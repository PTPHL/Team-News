28-6-2018 Simon Després frustrated
Washington Capitals defenseman Simon Després feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a regular player.

A native of Canada, Simon Després was originally drafted to the NHL by the Pittsburgh Penguins in the 1st round of the 2009 entry draft. He has appeared in 220 NHL games during his career, notching 6 goals and adding 39 assists along with 170 penalty minutes. The 26 year old defenseman is waiting for a good start this season.
