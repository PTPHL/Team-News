Brett Connolly marked a new career high in assists with 20 assists after assisting in tonights game against Anaheim Ducks on a goal by Tom Wilson. My kids attended this game so it is extra special for me, he said to the press.

A native of Canada, Brett Connolly was originally drafted to the NHL by the Tampa Bay Lightning in the 1st round of the 2010 entry draft. He has appeared in 630 NHL games during his career, notching 82 goals and adding 105 assists along with 333 penalty minutes. The 29 year old left wing has played 52 games with the Washington Capitals this season, totalling 11+20=31 with 16 PIM.
