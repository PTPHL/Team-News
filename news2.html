15-6-2019 Doyle Somerby frustrated
Columbus Blue Jackets defenseman Doyle Somerby feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of USA, Doyle Somerby was originally drafted to the NHL by the New York Islanders in the 5th round of the 2012 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 24 year old defenseman has not yet played for the Columbus Blue Jackets this season. He has played 82 games with the Cleveland Monsters this season, totalling 4+14=18 with 24 PIM.
