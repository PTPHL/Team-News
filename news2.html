8-3-2020 Boone Jenner frustrated
Columbus Blue Jackets center Boone Jenner feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Boone Jenner was originally drafted to the NHL by the Winnipeg Jets in the 2nd round of the 2011 entry draft. He has appeared in 482 NHL games during his career, notching 110 goals and adding 101 assists along with 283 penalty minutes. The 26 year old center has played 67 games with the Columbus Blue Jackets this season, totalling 11+13=24 with 39 PIM.
