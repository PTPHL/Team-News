3-1-2023 Connor McClennon reaches career high !
Connor McClennon marked a new career high in assists with 22 assists after assisting in tonights game against Minnesota Wild on a goal by Mattias Samuelsson. For me this is dream come true, he said to the press.

A native of Canada, Connor McClennon was originally drafted to the NHL by the Columbus Blue Jackets in the 1st round of the 2020 entry draft. He has appeared in 120 NHL games during his career, notching 37 goals and adding 43 assists along with 77 penalty minutes. The 20 year old right wing has played 38 games with the Columbus Blue Jackets this season, totalling 12+22=34 with 43 PIM.
