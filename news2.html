10-5-2020 Sonny Milano frustrated
Columbus Blue Jackets right wing Sonny Milano feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of USA, Sonny Milano was originally drafted to the NHL by the Winnipeg Jets in the 1st round of the 2014 entry draft. He has appeared in 232 NHL games during his career, notching 27 goals and adding 49 assists along with 57 penalty minutes. The 23 year old right wing has played 82 games with the Columbus Blue Jackets this season, totalling 12+27=39 with 45 PIM.
