4-11-2018 Cam Atkinson reaches a milestone !
Cam Atkinson scored the 300th point of his career in tonight's game against the Boston Bruins on a goal by Jamie Benn. I don't know why exactly, but the ice just seems to be more wide open for me this year, were his words after the game..

A native of USA, Cam Atkinson was originally drafted to the NHL by the Winnipeg Jets in the 6th round of the 2008 entry draft. He has appeared in 474 NHL games during his career, notching 150 goals and adding 150 assists along with 172 penalty minutes. The 29 year old right wing has played 10 games with the Columbus Blue Jackets this season, totalling 6+5=11 with 8 PIM.
3-11-2018 Doyle Somerby frustrated
Columbus Blue Jackets defenseman Doyle Somerby feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of USA, Doyle Somerby was originally drafted to the NHL by the New York Islanders in the 5th round of the 2012 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 24 year old defenseman has not yet played for the Columbus Blue Jackets this season. He has played 10 games with the Cleveland Monsters this season, totalling 0+2=2 with 12 PIM.
