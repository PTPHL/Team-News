4-11-2018 Mattias Janmark frustrated
Dallas Stars left wing Mattias Janmark feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a regular player.

A native of Sweden, Mattias Janmark was originally drafted to the NHL by the Detroit Red Wings in the 3rd round of the 2013 entry draft. He has appeared in 169 NHL games during his career, notching 28 goals and adding 36 assists along with 66 penalty minutes. The 25 year old left wing has played 14 games with the Dallas Stars this season, totalling 1+0=1 with 0 PIM.
