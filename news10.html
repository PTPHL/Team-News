10-3-2019 Nikita Filatov reaches career high !
Nikita Filatov marked a new career high in points with 39 after assisting in tonights game against Colorado Avalanche on a goal by Brett Ritchie. I've seen my idols break records and now I am doing the same, was the comment released to the press after the game..

A native of Russia, Nikita Filatov was originally drafted to the NHL by the Winnipeg Jets in the 1st round of the 2008 entry draft. He has appeared in 197 NHL games during his career, notching 34 goals and adding 57 assists along with 135 penalty minutes. The 28 year old left wing has played 67 games with the Dallas Stars this season, totalling 16+23=39 with 73 PIM.
