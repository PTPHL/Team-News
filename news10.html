31-8-2019 Nikita Filatov frustrated
Dallas Stars left wing Nikita Filatov feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a regular player.

A native of Russia, Nikita Filatov was originally drafted to the NHL by the Winnipeg Jets in the 1st round of the 2008 entry draft. He has appeared in 212 NHL games during his career, notching 40 goals and adding 60 assists along with 147 penalty minutes. The 29 year old left wing is waiting for a good start this season.
