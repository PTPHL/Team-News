A native of Czech, Ondrej Kase was originally drafted to the NHL by the Anaheim Ducks in the 7th round of the 2014 entry draft. The 24 year old left wing has not yet played for the Anaheim Ducks this season. He has played 67 games with the San Diego Gulls this season, totalling 20+17=37 with 20 PIM.
