6-3-2020 Patrick Kudla frustrated
Detroit Red Wings defenseman Patrick Kudla feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Patrick Kudla was originally drafted to the NHL by the Vegas Golden Knights in the 6th round of the 2016 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 23 year old defenseman has not yet played for the Detroit Red Wings this season.
