9-6-2020 Detroit Red Wings celebrate Stanley Cup
The Stanley Cup Champions Detroit Red Wings were celebrated in their hometown with grand festivities today. The Stanley Cup was driven through the town in a parade and the fans were chanting the team's name every time captain Ryan Kesler lifted the Cup above his head.

The mayor of the town greeted the players on a stage with the media surrounding them as masses of loyal Detroit Red Wings fans chanted the names of Pontus Aberg and Tuukka Rask.

Next year, we're doing this again, said the confident team captain Ryan Kesler for the reporters.
8-6-2020 Playoff news
Detroit Red Wings have won their series against the Carolina Hurricanes. They have been awarded $4,000,000 as prize money.
7-6-2020 Christoffer Ehn frustrated
Detroit Red Wings left wing Christoffer Ehn feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Sweden, Christoffer Ehn was originally drafted to the NHL by the Detroit Red Wings in the 4th round of the 2014 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 24 year old left wing has not yet played for the Detroit Red Wings this season. He has played 23 games with the Grand Rapids Griffins this season, totalling 2+1=3 with 2 PIM.
