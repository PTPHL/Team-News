12-5-2019 Dylan Sadowy frustrated
Detroit Red Wings left wing Dylan Sadowy feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Dylan Sadowy was originally drafted to the NHL by the San Jose Sharks in the 3rd round of the 2014 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 23 year old left wing has not yet played for the Detroit Red Wings this season. He has played 82 games with the Grand Rapids Griffins this season, totalling 34+22=56 with 12 PIM.
12-5-2019 DET Scotty Bowman praises Nolan Foote
Detroit Red Wings head coach DET Scotty Bowman has praised young left wing Nolan Foote in his latest interviews. Altough he seems very fond of this highly skilled youngster, he didn't want to comment on his team's strategy concerning the upcoming draft.

Nolan Foote has played 75 games with Kelowna Rockets this season, collecting 53+24=77 with 65 PIM.
