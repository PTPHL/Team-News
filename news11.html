8-4-2021 Sven Baertschi frustrated
Detroit Red Wings left wing Sven Baertschi feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a regular player.

A native of Switzerland, Sven Baertschi was originally drafted to the NHL by the Calgary Flames in the 1st round of the 2011 entry draft. He has appeared in 447 NHL games during his career, notching 91 goals and adding 109 assists along with 299 penalty minutes. The 28 year old left wing has played 71 games with the Detroit Red Wings this season, totalling 9+14=23 with 86 PIM. He has also played 7 games with the Grand Rapids Griffins this season, totalling 3+2=5 with 0 PIM.
