1-5-2020 Player of the month: Pontus Aberg (R)

A native of Sweden, Pontus Aberg was originally drafted to the NHL by the Nashville Predators in the 2nd round of the 2012 entry draft. He has appeared in 218 NHL games during his career, notching 41 goals and adding 74 assists along with 114 penalty minutes. The 26 year old right wing has played 82 games with the Detroit Red Wings this season, totalling 23+38=61 with 40 PIM.
