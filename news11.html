6-12-2018 Evgeni Svechnikov reaches career high !
Evgeni Svechnikov marked a new career high in assists with 14 assists after assisting in tonights game against Toronto Maple Leafs on a goal by Pontus Aberg. Making beautiful plays is fun on the ice I think, was all he could say after the game.

A native of Russia, Evgeni Svechnikov was originally drafted to the NHL by the Detroit Red Wings in the 1st round of the 2015 entry draft. He has appeared in 57 NHL games during his career, notching 15 goals and adding 27 assists along with 34 penalty minutes. The 22 year old left wing has played 28 games with the Detroit Red Wings this season, totalling 9+14=23 with 22 PIM.
