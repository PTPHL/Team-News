7-1-2022 Christian Dvorak reaches a milestone !
Christian Dvorak scored the 100th goal of his career in tonight's game against the Minnesota Wild on a feed by Anthony DeAngelo. We played some good, fundamental hockey tonight. When you do that the goals always follow, he pointed out to the gathered press corps.

A native of USA, Christian Dvorak was originally drafted to the NHL by the Vegas Golden Knights in the 2nd round of the 2014 entry draft. He has appeared in 449 NHL games during his career, notching 100 goals and adding 155 assists along with 143 penalty minutes. The 25 year old center has played 43 games with the Detroit Red Wings this season, totalling 9+18=27 with 2 PIM.
