7-4-2020 Christian Dvorak reaches career high !
Christian Dvorak marked a new career high in assists with 34 assists after assisting in tonights game against Colorado Avalanche on a goal by Ryan Suter. You gotta' hand it to their team today, they came to play and made us work for everything we got, the happy player commented.

A native of USA, Christian Dvorak was originally drafted to the NHL by the Vegas Golden Knights in the 2nd round of the 2014 entry draft. He has appeared in 323 NHL games during his career, notching 76 goals and adding 112 assists along with 100 penalty minutes. The 24 year old center has played 81 games with the Detroit Red Wings this season, totalling 22+34=56 with 22 PIM.
7-4-2020 Anthony Mantha reaches career high !
Anthony Mantha marked a new career high in assists with 29 assists after assisting in tonights game against Colorado Avalanche on a goal by Ryan Suter. I'm just happy to contribute, he replied after giving his game stick to a representative of a local charity organization.

A native of Canada, Anthony Mantha was originally drafted to the NHL by the Detroit Red Wings in the 1st round of the 2013 entry draft. The 25 year old left wing has played 81 games with the Detroit Red Wings this season, totalling 24+29=53 with 39 PIM.
