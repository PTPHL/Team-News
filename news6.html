8-4-2022 David Pastrnak reaches career high !
David Pastrnak marked a new career high in assists with 37 assists after assisting in tonights game against Tampa Bay Lightning on a goal by Julien Gauthier. Awesome, even more so because the game was televised, he commented and was all smiles.

A native of Czech, David Pastrnak was originally drafted to the NHL by the Boston Bruins in the 1st round of the 2014 entry draft. He has appeared in 583 NHL games during his career, notching 198 goals and adding 226 assists along with 338 penalty minutes. The 25 year old right wing has played 81 games with the Carolina Hurricanes this season, totalling 38+37=75 with 42 PIM.
