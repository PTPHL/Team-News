10-5-2019 Roland McKeown frustrated
Carolina Hurricanes defenseman Roland McKeown feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Roland McKeown was originally drafted to the NHL by the Los Angeles Kings in the 2nd round of the 2014 entry draft. He has appeared in 82 NHL games during his career, notching 8 goals and adding 24 assists along with 8 penalty minutes. The 23 year old defenseman has played 82 games with the Carolina Hurricanes this season, totalling 8+24=32 with 8 PIM.

Carolina Hurricanes head coach CAR Head Coach has praised young left wing Luke Toporowski in his latest interviews. Altough he seems very fond of this highly skilled youngster, he didn't want to comment on his team's strategy concerning the upcoming draft.
