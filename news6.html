26-6-2019 Elias Lindholm frustrated
Carolina Hurricanes center Elias Lindholm feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Sweden, Elias Lindholm was originally drafted to the NHL by the Carolina Hurricanes in the 1st round of the 2013 entry draft. He has appeared in 457 NHL games during his career, notching 84 goals and adding 138 assists along with 130 penalty minutes. The 24 year old center is waiting for a good start this season.
