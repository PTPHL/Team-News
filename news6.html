14-2-2019 Clayton Keller reaches career high !
Clayton Keller marked a new career high in assists with 22 assists after assisting in tonights game against Detroit Red Wings on a goal by Sebastian Aho. I've been fortunate enough to play with many great players and I could not have done it without them, he said as he packed up his gear.

A native of USA, Clayton Keller was originally drafted to the NHL by the Vegas Golden Knights in the 1st round of the 2016 entry draft. He has appeared in 139 NHL games during his career, notching 30 goals and adding 45 assists along with 20 penalty minutes. The 20 year old center has played 54 games with the Carolina Hurricanes this season, totalling 15+22=37 with 6 PIM.
