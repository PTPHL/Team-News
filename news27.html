27-1-2019 Dylan Strome reaches career high !
Dylan Strome marked a new career high in points with 30 after assisting in tonights game against Buffalo Sabres on a goal by Jaden Schwartz. It is just part of my job, he said to the press..

A native of Canada, Dylan Strome was originally drafted to the NHL by the Vegas Golden Knights in the 1st round of the 2015 entry draft. He has appeared in 130 NHL games during his career, notching 25 goals and adding 35 assists along with 39 penalty minutes. The 21 year old left wing has played 49 games with the Tampa Bay Lightning this season, totalling 10+20=30 with 27 PIM.
