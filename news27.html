1-1-2023 Player of the month: Jaden Schwartz (C)
NHL has awarded Jaden Schwartz (C) of Tampa Bay Lightning as Player Of The Month He registered 12 goals and 10 assists for 22 points in 16 games last month. He also had 2 game winning goals.

A native of Canada, Jaden Schwartz was originally drafted to the NHL by the St. Louis Blues in the 1st round of the 2010 entry draft. He has appeared in 764 NHL games during his career, notching 232 goals and adding 313 assists along with 320 penalty minutes. The 30 year old center has played 38 games with the Tampa Bay Lightning this season, totalling 19+19=38 with 10 PIM.
