17-2-2019 Victor Hedman reaches a milestone !
Victor Hedman notched the 300th assist of his career in tonight's game against the Buffalo Sabres on a goal by Kyle Connor. You gotta' hand it to their team today, they came to play and made us work for everything we got, he said after the game..

A native of Sweden, Victor Hedman was originally drafted to the NHL by the Tampa Bay Lightning in the 1st round of the 2009 entry draft. He has appeared in 685 NHL games during his career, notching 81 goals and adding 300 assists along with 534 penalty minutes. The 28 year old defenseman has played 54 games with the Tampa Bay Lightning this season, totalling 4+22=26 with 52 PIM.
