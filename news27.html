12-6-2019 Tampa Bay Lightning celebrate Stanley Cup
The Stanley Cup Champions Tampa Bay Lightning were celebrated in their hometown with grand festivities today. The Stanley Cup was driven through the town in a parade and the fans were chanting the team's name every time captain Sidney Crosby lifted the Cup above his head.

The mayor of the town greeted the players on a stage with the media surrounding them as masses of loyal Tampa Bay Lightning fans chanted the names of Sidney Crosby and Andrei Vasilevski.

This is as good as it gets, said Sidney Crosby amidst the festivities.
