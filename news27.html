17-6-2020 Matthew Peca frustrated
Tampa Bay Lightning center Matthew Peca feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a regular player.

A native of Canada, Matthew Peca was originally drafted to the NHL by the Tampa Bay Lightning in the 7th round of the 2011 entry draft. He has appeared in 19 NHL games during his career, notching 1 goals and adding 1 assists along with 4 penalty minutes. The 27 year old center has not yet played for the Tampa Bay Lightning this season. He has played 82 games with the Syracuse Crunch this season, totalling 10+22=32 with 32 PIM.
