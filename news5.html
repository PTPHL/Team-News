10-6-2022 Calgary Flames celebrate Stanley Cup
The Stanley Cup Champions Calgary Flames were celebrated in their hometown with grand festivities today. The Stanley Cup was driven through the town in a parade and the fans were chanting the team's name every time captain Sean Monahan lifted the Cup above his head.

The mayor of the town greeted the players on a stage with the media surrounding them as masses of loyal Calgary Flames fans chanted the names of Sean Monahan and Jon Gillies.

Next year, we're doing this again, said the confident team captain Sean Monahan for the reporters.
