3-6-2020 Oliver Kylington frustrated
Calgary Flames defenseman Oliver Kylington feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Sweden, Oliver Kylington was originally drafted to the NHL by the Calgary Flames in the 2nd round of the 2015 entry draft. He has appeared in 165 NHL games during his career, notching 12 goals and adding 62 assists along with 76 penalty minutes. The 23 year old defenseman has played 82 games with the Calgary Flames this season, totalling 6+38=44 with 61 PIM.
