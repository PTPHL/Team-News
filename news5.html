5-5-2021 Matthew Tkachuk frustrated
Calgary Flames right wing Matthew Tkachuk feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of USA, Matthew Tkachuk was originally drafted to the NHL by the Calgary Flames in the 1st round of the 2016 entry draft. He has appeared in 404 NHL games during his career, notching 99 goals and adding 136 assists along with 321 penalty minutes. The 23 year old right wing has played 82 games with the Calgary Flames this season, totalling 17+20=37 with 94 PIM.
