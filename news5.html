NHL has awarded Lucas Raymond (R) of Calgary Flames as Player Of The Week He registered 3 goals and 2 assists for 5 points in 3 games last week. He also had 1 game winning goal.

A native of Sweden, Lucas Raymond was originally drafted to the NHL by the New Jersey Devils in the 1st round of the 2020 entry draft. He has appeared in 164 NHL games during his career, notching 50 goals and adding 58 assists along with 131 penalty minutes. The 21 year old right wing has played 82 games with the Calgary Flames this season, totalling 24+31=55 with 48 PIM.
