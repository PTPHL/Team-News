10-12-2021 Player of the week: Lucas Raymond (R)
NHL has awarded Lucas Raymond (R) of Calgary Flames as Player Of The Week He registered 2 goals and 2 assists for 4 points in 3 games last week. He also had 2 game winning goals.

A native of Sweden, Lucas Raymond was originally drafted to the NHL by the New Jersey Devils in the 1st round of the 2020 entry draft. He has appeared in 31 NHL games during his career, notching 10 goals and adding 10 assists along with 35 penalty minutes. The 19 year old right wing has played 31 games with the Calgary Flames this season, totalling 10+10=20 with 35 PIM.
