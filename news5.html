1-1-2022 Rookie of the month: Lucas Raymond (R)
NHL has awarded Lucas Raymond (R) of Calgary Flames as Rookie Of The Month He registered 7 goals and 4 assists for 11 points in 12 games last month. He also had 4 game winning goals.

A native of Sweden, Lucas Raymond was originally drafted to the NHL by the New Jersey Devils in the 1st round of the 2020 entry draft. He has appeared in 39 NHL games during his career, notching 15 goals and adding 12 assists along with 37 penalty minutes. The 19 year old right wing has played 39 games with the Calgary Flames this season, totalling 15+12=27 with 37 PIM.
