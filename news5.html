15-6-2019 Calgary Flames trade
Calgary Flames traded Mark Giordano to Vancouver Canucks for Adam Gaudette

A native of USA, Adam Gaudette was originally drafted to the NHL by the Vancouver Canucks in the 5th round of the 2015 entry draft. He has appeared in 95 NHL games during his career, notching 11 goals and adding 22 assists along with 70 penalty minutes. The 22 year old center has played 82 games with the Vancouver Canucks this season, totalling 9+19=28 with 61 PIM.

A native of Canada, Mark Giordano, was originally signed as a free agent to the NHL. He has appeared in 837 NHL games during his career, notching 103 goals and adding 290 assists along with 646 penalty minutes. The 35 year old defenseman has played 82 games with the Calgary Flames this season, totalling 1+25=26 with 39 PIM.
