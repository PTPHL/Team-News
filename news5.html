Jeff Skinner scored the 400th point of his career in tonight's game against the Edmonton Oilers on a feed by TJ Brodie. There's no 'I' in team and we have to get everyone playing the same system. It's not enough to just play a solid 20 or 40 minutes of hockey - you have to play 60. Hey, you know what? I forgot the question, he commented to the media.

A native of Canada, Jeff Skinner was originally drafted to the NHL by the Carolina Hurricanes in the 1st round of the 2010 entry draft. The 26 year old left wing has played 17 games with the Calgary Flames this season, totalling 7+11=18 with 6 PIM.
