14-3-2022 Kieffer Bellows reaches career high !
Kieffer Bellows marked a new career high in goals with 22 tallies after scoring in tonights game against Winnipeg Jets on a feed by Lucas Raymond. It's always nice score but this was one special goal for me, was his only statement before going to the dressing room.

A native of USA, Kieffer Bellows was originally drafted to the NHL by the New York Islanders in the 1st round of the 2016 entry draft. He has appeared in 260 NHL games during his career, notching 70 goals and adding 102 assists along with 532 penalty minutes. The 23 year old left wing has played 68 games with the Calgary Flames this season, totalling 22+28=50 with 101 PIM.
