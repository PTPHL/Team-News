8-12-2018 Sean Monahan reaches a milestone !
Sean Monahan scored the 300th point of his career in tonight's game against the Anaheim Ducks on a goal by Alexander Steen. Wow, I feel good about it...I guess, he commented during a post game radio interview..

A native of Canada, Sean Monahan was originally drafted to the NHL by the Calgary Flames in the 1st round of the 2013 entry draft. He has appeared in 429 NHL games during his career, notching 143 goals and adding 157 assists along with 74 penalty minutes. The 24 year old center has played 28 games with the Calgary Flames this season, totalling 9+13=22 with 6 PIM.
