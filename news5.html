10-1-2019 Austin Carroll injured.
Austin Carroll of Stockton Heat suffered injury during tonights minorleague game against the Syracuse Crunch. He is expected to be sidelined for about a week . I just hope I can be able to return to action within a week, he commented in a radio interview after the game.
10-1-2019 Matthew Tkachuk injured.
Matthew Tkachuk (CAL) suffered wrist injury during the game in an incident with Victor Hedman (TBL). He is expected to be sidelined for a couple of days. Sometimes you just have bad luck and get knocked around, the injured player commented.
6-1-2019 Mark Jankowski reaches career high !
Mark Jankowski marked a new career high in points with 16 after assisting in tonights game against St. Louis Blues on a goal by Matthew Tkachuk. There's no 'I' in team and we have to get everyone playing the same system. It's not enough to just play a solid 20 or 40 minutes of hockey - you have to play 60. Hey, you know what? I forgot the question, he said after signing autographs for some kids..

A native of Canada, Mark Jankowski was originally drafted to the NHL by the Calgary Flames in the 1st round of the 2012 entry draft. He has appeared in 68 NHL games during his career, notching 14 goals and adding 17 assists along with 26 penalty minutes. The 24 year old center has played 40 games with the Calgary Flames this season, totalling 8+8=16 with 14 PIM.
