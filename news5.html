27-6-2018 Brett Kulak frustrated
Calgary Flames defenseman Brett Kulak feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a regular player.

A native of Canada, Brett Kulak was originally drafted to the NHL by the Calgary Flames in the 4th round of the 2012 entry draft. He has appeared in 37 NHL games during his career, notching 0 goals and adding 4 assists along with 14 penalty minutes. The 24 year old defenseman is expecting a good season for the team.
