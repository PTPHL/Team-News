4-4-2019 Jeff Skinner reaches career high !
Jeff Skinner marked a new career high in assists with 33 assists after assisting in tonights game against Edmonton Oilers on a goal by Kieffer Bellows. Give all the credit to the goalscorer, he joked.

A native of Canada, Jeff Skinner was originally drafted to the NHL by the Carolina Hurricanes in the 1st round of the 2010 entry draft. He has appeared in 657 NHL games during his career, notching 228 goals and adding 212 assists along with 305 penalty minutes. The 26 year old center has played 79 games with the Calgary Flames this season, totalling 25+33=58 with 43 PIM.
4-4-2019 Hunter Shinkaruk reaches career high !
Hunter Shinkaruk marked a new career high in goals with 21 tallies after scoring in tonights game against Edmonton Oilers on a feed by Mark Giordano. I just try and give it my best out there. This one was special though, he pointed out to the gathered press corps.

A native of Canada, Hunter Shinkaruk was originally drafted to the NHL by the Vancouver Canucks in the 1st round of the 2013 entry draft. He has appeared in 149 NHL games during his career, notching 43 goals and adding 49 assists along with 49 penalty minutes. The 24 year old center has played 79 games with the Calgary Flames this season, totalling 21+25=46 with 27 PIM.
2-4-2019 Johnny Gaudreau reaches career high !
Johnny Gaudreau marked a new career high in goals with 31 tallies after scoring in tonights game against Vegas Golden Knights on a feed by Hunter Shinkaruk. Hopefully one day this puck will end up in the Hall of Fame, he said after the game.

A native of USA, Johnny Gaudreau was originally drafted to the NHL by the Calgary Flames in the 4th round of the 2011 entry draft. He has appeared in 383 NHL games during his career, notching 126 goals and adding 194 assists along with 145 penalty minutes. The 25 year old right wing has played 78 games with the Calgary Flames this season, totalling 31+35=66 with 71 PIM.
