25-6-2019 Alan Quine frustrated
New York Islanders center Alan Quine feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a regular player.

A native of Canada, Alan Quine was originally drafted to the NHL by the New York Islanders in the 6th round of the 2013 entry draft. He has appeared in 63 NHL games during his career, notching 6 goals and adding 13 assists along with 8 penalty minutes. The 26 year old center is eagerly waiting to hit the ice once the season starts.

21-6-2019 Sebastian Aho frustrated
New York Islanders defenseman Sebastian Aho feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.
