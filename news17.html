7-3-2020 Derrick Pouliot reaches career high !
Derrick Pouliot marked a new career high in points with 23 after assisting in tonights game against Carolina Hurricanes on a goal by Artem Anisimov. In a game like this, it was nice to set that milestone, was his only comment for the media..

A native of Canada, Derrick Pouliot was originally drafted to the NHL by the Pittsburgh Penguins in the 1st round of the 2012 entry draft. He has appeared in 272 NHL games during his career, notching 11 goals and adding 58 assists along with 82 penalty minutes. The 26 year old defenseman has played 65 games with the New York Islanders this season, totalling 2+21=23 with 24 PIM.

2-3-2020 Robin Kovacs frustrated
New York Islanders right wing Robin Kovacs feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Sweden, Robin Kovacs was originally drafted to the NHL by the New York Rangers in the 3rd round of the 2015 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 23 year old right wing has not yet played for the New York Islanders this season. He has played 29 games with the Bridgeport Sound Tigers this season, totalling 2+2=4 with 4 PIM.
