6-5-2023 Marcus Davidsson frustrated
New York Islanders center Marcus Davidsson feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Sweden, Marcus Davidsson was originally drafted to the NHL by the Buffalo Sabres in the 2nd round of the 2017 entry draft. He has appeared in 178 NHL games during his career, notching 8 goals and adding 12 assists along with 64 penalty minutes. The 24 year old center has not yet played for the New York Islanders this season. He has played 73 games with the Bridgeport Sound Tigers this season, totalling 29+67=96 with 22 PIM.
