4-4-2020 Anthony Beauvillier reaches career high !
Anthony Beauvillier marked a new career high in points with 41 after scoring in tonights game against Tampa Bay Lightning on a feed by Olli Juolevi. That's one for the books, he commented and was all smiles.

A native of Canada, Anthony Beauvillier was originally drafted to the NHL by the New York Islanders in the 1st round of the 2015 entry draft. He has appeared in 309 NHL games during his career, notching 56 goals and adding 80 assists along with 255 penalty minutes. The 22 year old left wing has played 79 games with the New York Islanders this season, totalling 18+23=41 with 60 PIM.
