5-3-2023 Sasha Mutala reaches career high !
Sasha Mutala marked a new career high in points with 51 after scoring in tonights game against New York Rangers on a feed by Josh Doan. Yes, it does feel great, our record breaker told a Hockey Night in Canada reporter.

A native of Canada, Sasha Mutala was originally drafted to the NHL by the Montreal Canadiens in the 1st round of the 2019 entry draft. He has appeared in 249 NHL games during his career, notching 71 goals and adding 85 assists along with 209 penalty minutes. The 21 year old right wing has played 66 games with the New York Islanders this season, totalling 24+27=51 with 34 PIM.
