3-9-2020 Devante Stephens frustrated
Buffalo Sabres defenseman Devante Stephens feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Devante Stephens was originally drafted to the NHL by the Buffalo Sabres in the 5th round of the 2015 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 23 year old defenseman is eagerly waiting to hit the ice once the season starts.
2-9-2020 Sam Reinhart frustrated
Buffalo Sabres right wing Sam Reinhart feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a franchise player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Sam Reinhart was originally drafted to the NHL by the Buffalo Sabres in the 1st round of the 2014 entry draft. He has appeared in 413 NHL games during his career, notching 100 goals and adding 134 assists along with 64 penalty minutes. The 24 year old right wing is looking forward for the start of the season.
