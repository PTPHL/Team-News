7-12-2018 Jonas Johansson frustrated
Buffalo Sabres goaltender Jonas Johansson feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Sweden, Jonas Johansson was originally drafted to the NHL by the Buffalo Sabres in the 3rd round of the 2014 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 23 year old goaltender has not yet played for the Buffalo Sabres this season.
