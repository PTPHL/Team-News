9-6-2023 Ryan Nugent-Hopkins frustrated
Buffalo Sabres center Ryan Nugent-Hopkins feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a franchise player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a core player.

A native of Canada, Ryan Nugent-Hopkins was originally drafted to the NHL by the Edmonton Oilers in the 1st round of the 2011 entry draft. He has appeared in 887 NHL games during his career, notching 224 goals and adding 348 assists along with 344 penalty minutes. The 30 year old center has played 82 games with the Buffalo Sabres this season, totalling 24+41=65 with 34 PIM.
8-6-2023 Otto Somppi frustrated
