16-6-2019 Hudson Fasching frustrated
Buffalo Sabres left wing Hudson Fasching feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of USA, Hudson Fasching was originally drafted to the NHL by the Los Angeles Kings in the 4th round of the 2013 entry draft. He has appeared in 119 NHL games during his career, notching 8 goals and adding 13 assists along with 42 penalty minutes. The 23 year old left wing has played 82 games with the Buffalo Sabres this season, totalling 3+7=10 with 28 PIM.
