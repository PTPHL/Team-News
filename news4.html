7-2-2022 Robby Fabbri reaches a milestone !
Robby Fabbri scored the 300th point of his career in tonight's game against the New York Rangers on a goal by Alex Pietrangelo. In a game like this, it was nice to set that milestone, our record breaker told a Hockey Night in Canada reporter..

A native of Canada, Robby Fabbri was originally drafted to the NHL by the St. Louis Blues in the 1st round of the 2014 entry draft. He has appeared in 486 NHL games during his career, notching 129 goals and adding 171 assists along with 366 penalty minutes. The 26 year old right wing has played 54 games with the Buffalo Sabres this season, totalling 22+18=40 with 37 PIM.
