28-6-2019 Ty Rattie frustrated
Montreal Canadiens right wing Ty Rattie feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a regular player.

A native of Canada, Ty Rattie was originally drafted to the NHL by the St. Louis Blues in the 2nd round of the 2011 entry draft. He has appeared in 142 NHL games during his career, notching 15 goals and adding 21 assists along with 56 penalty minutes. The 26 year old right wing is waiting for a good start this season.
26-6-2019 Zachary Fucale frustrated
Montreal Canadiens goaltender Zachary Fucale feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Zachary Fucale was originally drafted to the NHL by the Montreal Canadiens in the 2nd round of the 2013 entry draft. He has appeared in 14 NHL games during his career, booking 0 career shutouts and allowing 37 goals. The 24 year old goaltender is looking forward for the start of the season.
