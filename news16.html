7-6-2020 Jonathan Drouin frustrated
Montreal Canadiens center Jonathan Drouin feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a franchise player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Jonathan Drouin was originally drafted to the NHL by the Tampa Bay Lightning in the 1st round of the 2013 entry draft. He has appeared in 393 NHL games during his career, notching 92 goals and adding 159 assists along with 87 penalty minutes. The 25 year old center has played 82 games with the Montreal Canadiens this season, totalling 23+30=53 with 9 PIM.
5-6-2020 MTL Lew Lamoriello praises Daniil Gushchin
Montreal Canadiens head coach MTL Lew Lamoriello has praised young left wing Daniil Gushchin in his latest interviews. Altough he seems very fond of this highly skilled youngster, he didn't want to comment on his team's strategy concerning the upcoming draft.

Daniil Gushchin has played 61 games with Ak Bars Kazan this season, collecting 36+18=54 with 7 PIM.
1-6-2020 Giorgio Estephan frustrated
Montreal Canadiens center Giorgio Estephan feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Giorgio Estephan was originally drafted to the NHL by the Buffalo Sabres in the 6th round of the 2015 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level.
