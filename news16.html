5-5-2019 Charles Hudon frustrated
Montreal Canadiens left wing Charles Hudon feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a regular player.

A native of Canada, Charles Hudon was originally drafted to the NHL by the Montreal Canadiens in the 5th round of the 2012 entry draft. He has appeared in 75 NHL games during his career, notching 5 goals and adding 12 assists along with 48 penalty minutes. The 24 year old left wing has played 64 games with the Montreal Canadiens this season, totalling 5+7=12 with 42 PIM.

1-5-2019 Player of the month: Jonathan Huberdeau (C)
