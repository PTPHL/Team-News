4-2-2022 Jaden Schwartz reaches a milestone !
Jaden Schwartz scored the 200th goal of his career in tonight's game against the Buffalo Sabres on a feed by Nick Bjugstad. Hopefully one day this puck will end up in the Hall of Fame, he said outside the teams dressing room.

A native of Canada, Jaden Schwartz was originally drafted to the NHL by the St. Louis Blues in the 1st round of the 2010 entry draft. He has appeared in 699 NHL games during his career, notching 200 goals and adding 276 assists along with 298 penalty minutes. The 29 year old left wing has played 55 games with the Montreal Canadiens this season, totalling 24+27=51 with 37 PIM.
