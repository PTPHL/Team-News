17-11-2018 Jonathan Huberdeau reaches a milestone !
Jonathan Huberdeau scored the 100th goal of his career in tonight's game against the Calgary Flames on a feed by Kevin Connauton. We played some good, fundamental hockey tonight. When you do that the goals always follow, he said after the game in the dressing room.

A native of Canada, Jonathan Huberdeau was originally drafted to the NHL by the Florida Panthers in the 1st round of the 2011 entry draft. He has appeared in 401 NHL games during his career, notching 100 goals and adding 182 assists along with 215 penalty minutes. The 25 year old center has played 16 games with the Montreal Canadiens this season, totalling 5+9=14 with 14 PIM.
