9-1-2019 Ty Rattie frustrated
Montreal Canadiens right wing Ty Rattie feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a regular player.

A native of Canada, Ty Rattie was originally drafted to the NHL by the St. Louis Blues in the 2nd round of the 2011 entry draft. He has appeared in 101 NHL games during his career, notching 9 goals and adding 17 assists along with 36 penalty minutes. The 25 year old right wing has played 41 games with the Montreal Canadiens this season, totalling 4+7=11 with 16 PIM.
