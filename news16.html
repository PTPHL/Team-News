16-6-2019 Daniel Audette frustrated
Montreal Canadiens left wing Daniel Audette feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of USA, Daniel Audette was originally drafted to the NHL by the Montreal Canadiens in the 5th round of the 2014 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 23 year old left wing has not yet played for the Montreal Canadiens this season. He has played 66 games with the Laval Rocket this season, totalling 23+13=36 with 2 PIM.
