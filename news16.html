6-11-2021 Joonas Lyytinen frustrated
Montreal Canadiens defenseman Joonas Lyytinen feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Finland, Joonas Lyytinen was originally drafted to the NHL by the Montreal Canadiens in the 4th round of the 2019 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 26 year old defenseman has not yet played for the Montreal Canadiens this season. He has played 14 games with the Laval Rocket this season, totalling 1+1=2 with 4 PIM.
