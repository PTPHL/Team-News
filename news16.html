17-6-2020 Montreal Canadiens trade
Montreal Canadiens traded Brandon Saad and William Bitten to Anaheim Ducks for Rickard Rakell

A native of Sweden, Rickard Rakell was originally drafted to the NHL by the Anaheim Ducks in the 1st round of the 2011 entry draft. He has appeared in 453 NHL games during his career, notching 117 goals and adding 149 assists along with 127 penalty minutes. The 27 year old left wing has played 82 games with the Anaheim Ducks this season, totalling 22+31=53 with 26 PIM.

A native of USA, Brandon Saad was originally drafted to the NHL by the Chicago Blackhawks in the 2nd round of the 2011 entry draft. He has appeared in 613 NHL games during his career, notching 182 goals and adding 217 assists along with 189 penalty minutes. The 27 year old left wing has played 82 games with the Montreal Canadiens this season, totalling 25+19=44 with 36 PIM.

A native of Canada, William Bitten was originally drafted to the NHL by the Montreal Canadiens in the 3rd round of the 2016 entry draft. He has appeared in 19 NHL games during his career, notching 3 goals and adding 3 assists along with 37 penalty minutes. The 21 year old right wing has played 19 games with the Montreal Canadiens this season, totalling 3+3=6 with 37 PIM. He has also played 53 games with the Laval Rocket this season, totalling 38+52=90 with 30 PIM.
