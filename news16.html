6-3-2022 Michael Del Zotto reaches a milestone !
Michael Del Zotto scored the 300th point of his career in tonight's game against the Los Angeles Kings on a goal by Sasha Mutala. All of them are special, but I'm going to remember this one the most, he said at the post-game press conference..

A native of Canada, Michael Del Zotto was originally drafted to the NHL by the New York Rangers in the 1st round of the 2008 entry draft. He has appeared in 829 NHL games during his career, notching 69 goals and adding 231 assists along with 317 penalty minutes. The 31 year old defenseman has played 66 games with the Montreal Canadiens this season, totalling 3+17=20 with 21 PIM.
6-3-2022 Jaden Schwartz reaches career high !
Jaden Schwartz marked a new career high in points with 64 after assisting in tonights game against Los Angeles Kings on a goal by Shea Weber. I've seen my idols break records and now I am doing the same, he replied before ducking into the dressing room..

A native of Canada, Jaden Schwartz was originally drafted to the NHL by the St. Louis Blues in the 1st round of the 2010 entry draft. He has appeared in 710 NHL games during his career, notching 206 goals and adding 283 assists along with 302 penalty minutes. The 29 year old left wing has played 66 games with the Montreal Canadiens this season, totalling 30+34=64 with 41 PIM.
