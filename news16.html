8-4-2019 Jonathan Drouin reaches career high !
Jonathan Drouin marked a new career high in assists with 33 assists after assisting in tonights game against Philadelphia Flyers on a goal by Brock Boeser. Making beautiful plays is fun on the ice I think, he said after viewing the play on the TV studio monitor.

A native of Canada, Jonathan Drouin was originally drafted to the NHL by the Tampa Bay Lightning in the 1st round of the 2013 entry draft. He has appeared in 310 NHL games during his career, notching 69 goals and adding 129 assists along with 78 penalty minutes. The 24 year old center has played 81 games with the Montreal Canadiens this season, totalling 25+33=58 with 2 PIM.
