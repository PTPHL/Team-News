5-4-2020 Giorgio Estephan frustrated
Montreal Canadiens center Giorgio Estephan feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Giorgio Estephan was originally drafted to the NHL by the Buffalo Sabres in the 6th round of the 2015 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 23 year old center has not yet played for the Montreal Canadiens this season. He has played 29 games with the Laval Rocket this season, totalling 2+12=14 with 4 PIM.
