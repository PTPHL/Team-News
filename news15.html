10-3-2019 Damon Severson reaches career high !
Damon Severson marked a new career high in goals with 6 tallies after scoring in tonights game against Pittsburgh Penguins on a feed by John Quenneville. It is just an amazing thing for me, he said as his teammates crowded around him.

A native of Canada, Damon Severson was originally drafted to the NHL by the New Jersey Devils in the 2nd round of the 2012 entry draft. He has appeared in 350 NHL games during his career, notching 18 goals and adding 110 assists along with 187 penalty minutes. The 24 year old defenseman has played 65 games with the Minnesota Wild this season, totalling 6+26=32 with 22 PIM.
