14-6-2020 Aaron Ekblad frustrated
Minnesota Wild defenseman Aaron Ekblad feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a franchise player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Aaron Ekblad was originally drafted to the NHL by the Florida Panthers in the 1st round of the 2014 entry draft. He has appeared in 472 NHL games during his career, notching 59 goals and adding 139 assists along with 267 penalty minutes. The 24 year old defenseman has played 81 games with the Minnesota Wild this season, totalling 7+32=39 with 48 PIM.
