27-6-2019 Devin Shore frustrated
Minnesota Wild center Devin Shore feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a regular player.

A native of Canada, Devin Shore was originally drafted to the NHL by the Dallas Stars in the 2nd round of the 2012 entry draft. He has appeared in 85 NHL games during his career, notching 13 goals and adding 20 assists along with 14 penalty minutes. The 24 year old center is eagerly waiting to hit the ice once the season starts.
