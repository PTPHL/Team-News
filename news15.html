11-5-2019 Brendan Perlini frustrated
Minnesota Wild right wing Brendan Perlini feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Brendan Perlini was originally drafted to the NHL by the Vegas Golden Knights in the 1st round of the 2014 entry draft. He has appeared in 165 NHL games during his career, notching 36 goals and adding 42 assists along with 52 penalty minutes. The 23 year old right wing has played 77 games with the Minnesota Wild this season, totalling 13+25=38 with 26 PIM.
