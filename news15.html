9-2-2019 Gustav Olofsson frustrated
Minnesota Wild defenseman Gustav Olofsson feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Sweden, Gustav Olofsson was originally drafted to the NHL by the Minnesota Wild in the 2nd round of the 2013 entry draft. He has appeared in 52 NHL games during his career, notching 2 goals and adding 5 assists along with 6 penalty minutes. The 24 year old defenseman has played 37 games with the Minnesota Wild this season, totalling 2+2=4 with 4 PIM.
