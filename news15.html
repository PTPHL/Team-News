7-6-2020 Devin Shore frustrated
Minnesota Wild center Devin Shore feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a regular player.

A native of Canada, Devin Shore was originally drafted to the NHL by the Dallas Stars in the 2nd round of the 2012 entry draft. He has appeared in 85 NHL games during his career, notching 13 goals and adding 20 assists along with 14 penalty minutes. The 25 year old center has not yet played for the Minnesota Wild this season. He has played 53 games with the Iowa Wild this season, totalling 24+51=75 with 20 PIM.
