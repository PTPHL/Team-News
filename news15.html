A native of Finland, Jesse Ylonen was originally drafted to the NHL by the Minnesota Wild in the 3rd round of the 2018 entry draft. He has appeared in 125 NHL games during his career, notching 16 goals and adding 21 assists along with 88 penalty minutes. The 23 year old right wing has played 34 games with the Minnesota Wild this season, totalling 6+4=10 with 14 PIM.
