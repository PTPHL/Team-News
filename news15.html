4-11-2022 Jesse Puljujärvi frustrated
Minnesota Wild right wing Jesse Puljujärvi feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a franchise player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Sweden, Jesse Puljujärvi was originally drafted to the NHL by the Edmonton Oilers in the 1st round of the 2016 entry draft. He has appeared in 422 NHL games during his career, notching 124 goals and adding 159 assists along with 137 penalty minutes. The 24 year old right wing has played 13 games with the Minnesota Wild this season, totalling 3+3=6 with 2 PIM.
