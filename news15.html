15-6-2019 Minnesota Wild trade
Minnesota Wild traded Noah Juulsen and Brady Tkachuk to Winnipeg Jets for Shea Theodore and Nikolaj Ehlers

The 23 year old defenseman has played 82 games with the Winnipeg Jets this season, totalling 9+26=35 with 22 PIM.

A native of La-la-land, Nikolaj Ehlers was originally drafted to the NHL by the Columbus Blue Jackets in the 1st round of the 2014 entry draft. He has appeared in 318 NHL games during his career, notching 98 goals and adding 124 assists along with 127 penalty minutes. The 23 year old right wing has played 82 games with the Winnipeg Jets this season, totalling 30+30=60 with 26 PIM.

A native of Canada, Noah Juulsen was originally drafted to the NHL by the Montreal Canadiens in the 1st round of the 2015 entry draft. He has appeared in 97 NHL games during his career, notching 5 goals and adding 20 assists along with 33 penalty minutes. The 22 year old defenseman has played 84 games with the Minnesota Wild this season, totalling 5+19=24 with 27 PIM.

A native of USA, Brady Tkachuk was originally drafted to the NHL by the Minnesota Wild in the 1st round of the 2018 entry draft. He has appeared in 20 NHL games during his career, notching 5 goals and adding 5 assists along with 14 penalty minutes. The 19 year old center has played 20 games with the Minnesota Wild this season, totalling 5+5=10 with 14 PIM. He has also played 62 games with the Iowa Wild this season, totalling 44+46=90 with 16 PIM.
