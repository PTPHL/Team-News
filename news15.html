7-4-2020 Anton Slepyshev reaches career high !
Anton Slepyshev marked a new career high in assists with 20 assists after assisting in tonights game against Vancouver Canucks on a goal by Adam Tambellini. Whatever I can do to help the team is okay by me. As long as we get the 2 points that's the main thing, he said while posing for photo's.

A native of Russia, Anton Slepyshev was originally drafted to the NHL by the Edmonton Oilers in the 3rd round of the 2013 entry draft. He has appeared in 271 NHL games during his career, notching 44 goals and adding 65 assists along with 126 penalty minutes. The 25 year old left wing has played 81 games with the Minnesota Wild this season, totalling 13+20=33 with 41 PIM.
